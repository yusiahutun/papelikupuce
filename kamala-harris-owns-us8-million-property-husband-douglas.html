Kamala Harris owns US$8 million of property with husband Douglas Emhoff look inside the Democrat

They live in Brentwood, Los Angeles, in a house that Emhoff bought in 2012 and that has since been transferred to a trust, according to the WSJ report.

According to online US real estate database Zillow, the property has four bedrooms and four-and-a-half bathrooms. The off-market listing says the home sits on 3,500 sq ft and estimates its value to be around US$5.4 million. The median home value in Brentwood is US$2.7 million, which is higher than the Los Angeles median.

Records on US real estate data provider PropertyShark indicate that the single-family home was built in 1948. While the actual home is blurred on Google Maps, the rest of the street appears to be lined with houses with slanted roofs and plenty of greenery.

Harris isn’t the first political figure to call Brentwood home. Richard Nixon lived in Brentwood in 1961 before he was president. During that time, a devastating fire scorched the Bel Air and Brentwood neighbourhoods, though the house Nixon was leasing was spared.

Additionally, Harris and Emhoff own a condo in the West End neighbourhood of Washington. WSJ reported that Harris bought the two-bedroom condo for US$1.7 million in 2017, the same year she joined the Senate.

Photos of condos in the high-end Westlight building show a sleek design and floor-to-ceiling windows that bathe the rooms with natural light. The flat building website lists amenities like a heated rooftop pool, a business centre and a fitness centre with Peloton equipment.

The condo building is within walking distance of DuPont circle. Per Zillow, the median home value in the West End neighbourhood is US$702,000 and the median rent is US$3,172.

When Harris was the District Attorney of San Francisco, she owned a loft-style apartment that she bought for just shy of half a million dollars. She assumed office as San Francisco’s District Attorney in 2004. The flat, which Harris bought the same year, is currently valued at US$890,000 – about double what she paid for it.
