Gavin Huston's Mini-Bio

Born on December 10, 1977, in Brooklyn, NYC, USA, Gavin Houston is an American actor best known for his role as Jeffrey Harrington on the Oprah Winfrey Network soap opera, "The Haves and the Have Nots."

He is the son of Lloyd Houston and Colette Houston. He also has an older sister named Tonza. 

He began his career as a child actor appearing in commercials that show his love for the industry from a very young age. He is skilled and is talented enough to earn every bit of success he deserves in his career.
