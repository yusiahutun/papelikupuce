Gwyneth Paltrow Wearing New Wedding Ring Featuring Brad Falchuks Initials

It seems as though Gwyneth Paltrow and Brad Falchuk maybe did pull off that secret wedding over the weekend, because Goop's creator is now wearing a new wedding band, which features Falchuk's initials.

Related Story

Appearing on the red carpet last night, wearing a chic retrofête Shira jumpsuit, Paltrow was wearing several rings, including a noticeable signet band featuring the initials "BF" on her ring finger.

Paltrow was attending the Pre-Broadway Opening of Head Over Heels, The Go-Go's musical which she is producing.

Getty Images

And the actress didn't try to hide the fact that she was wearing a sizeable new ring on that all-important finger, seemingly posing for photos with her new jewelry front and center:

Getty Images

Getty Images

However, not everyone is convinced that Paltrow and Falchuk are married just yet. Page Six is reporting that source called the event "just an over-the-top engagement party to celebrate the couple," with a wedding date in the Hamptons set for later this year.

Following rumors that she'd managed to have a secret wedding, hosted by American Horror Story and Glee co-creator Ryan Murphy, Paltrow appeared to confirm the news herself on Instagram. Sharing an adorable picture of Falchuk, she wrote:

"Thank you @mrrpmurphy for the most incredible evening to celebrate our ❤️. There will never be adequate words to express our gratitude. @giambattistavalliparis I will never get over my gorgeous dress.... Thank you to all of our amazing true and dear friends who came to support us. We feel like the luckiest people on the planet because we have all of you in our lives. And most of all thank you @bradfalchuk for the true happiness."

The "engagement party" was attended by a number of A-list celebrities, including Jennifer Aniston, Kate Hudson, Cameron Diaz, Julia Roberts, Liv Tyler, Reese Witherspoon, Demi Moore and Chelsea Handler. Paltrow's mom, Blythe Danner, was also spotted at the venue carrying a Monique Lhuillier bridal dress bag, which suggested that a wedding was taking place.
