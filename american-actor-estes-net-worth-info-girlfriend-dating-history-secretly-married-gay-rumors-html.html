Will Estes's Short Bio:

Will Estes was born on 22nd October 1978 in Los Angeles, California. He attended Santa Monica College and majored in English literature. In the year 1989, he got a major break in the television series "New Lassie" to play the role of Will McCollough.

Since then he has been starring in plenty of roles in tv series and in music videos of Bon Jo Vi" It's My Life, " and Meat Loaf's video "Objects In The Rear View Mirror Appear Closer Than They Are."

In his early years, he did appear in many commercials. He got his first movie break in the year 2000, for the movie U-571 for the role of Seaman Ronald 'Rabbit' Parker.

Similar to his personal life, Estes keeps his earnings a secret. The actor has not explicitly revealed the figure of his net worth, and by the looks of it, he does not seem to reveal it anytime soon!

Since 2010, Will has appeared on Blue Bloods. Apart from that, he has also performed on Anchor, Automotives, Magic Valley and more.
