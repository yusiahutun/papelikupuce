Revealed: The $149 4-in-1 skincare wand used by stars from Reese Witherspoon to Pedro Pascal

The Solawave anti-wrinkle light therapy gadget has so many celebrity fans

    If you’re wondering how the stars get ready for the red carpet, we can reveal a major beauty secret: a 4-in-1 skincare gadget with celebrity fans including Reese Witherspoon, Pedro Pascal, Megan Fox and Nicole Kidman.

    We might not be headed to a red carpet anytime soon, but we have to say that a single beauty device that helps tone our face and neck, boost radiance and reduce puffiness sounds pretty amazing.

    Of course, LED masks that help increase collagen and reduce the appearance of acne and wrinkles are a major skincare trend right now, along with at-home laser tools like Kim Kardashian’s favorite $2,695 LYMA device.

    $149 at Solawave

    But not only does the celebrity-approved Solawave take light therapy to the next level, combining it with three more treatments – microcurrent therapy, facial massage and therapeutic warmth –  it’s priced at $149, which is actually pretty affordable by Hollywood standards! 

    Keep an eye out on Instagram and we’re sure you’ll see one of the many estheticians and celebrity makeup artists who swear by the quirky device.

    The Last of Us star Pedro Pascal’s groomer Coco Ullrich-Mooney (@cocoullrich) shared an Instagram post revealing the actor got his glow on for the Oscars red carpet with the help of the tool. As one fan commented, “I keep seeing Solawave everywhere—it must be working because that skin was glowing. The hair was also suave as hell. Well done I say!”

    Megan Fox’s makeup artist Jenna Kristina (@jennakristina) used the wand to prep the Transformers star’s skin for the Tribeca Film Festival. Sharing her secrets on Instagram, the MUA wrote that the Solawave is one of her “favorite tools” to instantly brighten the skin. “I travel everywhere with mine.”
