400+ halfling names for your Dungeons &amp; Dragons characters

In the fantasy world of role-playing games like Dungeons & Dragon, halflings are portrayed as a fictional race of small humanoids with curly hair, a love for good food, and community. They are also known for their strong inclination towards trickery and thievery, as reflected in their names. But which common DnD halfling names should you consider for your character?

Halflings are the friendliest beings in the fantasy fictional world. Their bravery and curiosity are probably why you’ll want to use halflings as your DnD characters. However, it would help if you used a halfling name that would reflect their trait. So, which are the best names for halflings in DnD?

Halfling names for your Dungeons & Dragons

What should you consider when creating halfling names? Halfling monikers are melodic and can range from short to fairly long. While their surnames don’t consider gender, female and male halfling names have a visible difference.
