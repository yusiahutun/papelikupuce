Who is Samantha Mathis? Bio & Wiki

Samantha Mathis is an American actress who is well known for her roles in many popular movies and TV shows. She was born on 12th May 1970, in Brooklyn, New York City, New York, U.S. Moreover, she is an American by her nationality and belongs to white ethnicity. She is the daughter of Donald Mathis and Bibi Besch, an Australian-American actress. Unfortunately, her parents separated when she was only two years old.

After the divorce, Mathis and her mother, Bibi relocated to Los Angeles at the age of five. Her mother didn’t want Mathis to become an actress because she faced many problems in her acting career. But, she wanted to act due to her growing up in locations like in theaters, and in acting classes. Later, she decided to become an actress when she was twelve years old.

Who is Samantha Mathis’ Husband? Married & Children?

Untimely, Phoenix died at Cedars-Sinai Hospital because of a drug overdose after collapsing outside West Hollywood’s Viper Room on 31st October 1993. That night when Phoenix died, Mathis was with him. In an interview with The Guardian in 2018, she stated,

“I knew something was wrong that night, something I didn’t understand and i didn’t see anyone doing drugs but he was high in a way that made me feel uncomfortable…the heroin that killed him didn’t happen until he was in the Viper Room. I have my suspicions about what was going on, but I didn’t see anything.”
