Jack Grealish 'tries to chat up Love Island's Natalia Zoppa'- but he's called out by her boyfriend

Premier League footballer Jack Grealish reportedly tried to chat up Love Island beauty Natalia Zoppa on Instagram – before he was called out by her boyfriend.

The Aston Villa ace, 25, is said to have sent the reality beauty, 21, a direct message with a waving hand emoji, widely seen as code for an opening chat-up move.

However, the brunette's beau Hass Saleh soon made the message public, as he called out the England attacking midfielder in a hilarious TikTok video.
