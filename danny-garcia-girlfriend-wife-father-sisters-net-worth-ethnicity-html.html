Professional Career And Net Worth

As an amateur boxer, Danny participated in 2005 under-19 National Champion, 2005 Tammer Tournament Champion, and 2006 US National Champion. He began professional boxing in 2007 and eventually outcast all the boxers in Light welterweight including the professional boxer Amir Khan.

In mid-2015, he began his Welterweight boxing and eventually won with notable boxers such as Malignaggi, Guerrero, Vargas, Thurman, and Rios. He is now one of the Puerto Rican boxing champions.
