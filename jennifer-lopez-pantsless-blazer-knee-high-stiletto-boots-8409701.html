Jennifer Lopez Paired Her Pantsless Blazer Look With Knee-High Stiletto Boots

The pantsless trend has captured the hearts of many Hollywood darlings, from Hailey Bieber and Kendall Jenner to Sydney Sweeney. The latest A-lister to jump on the trend is none other than Jennifer Lopez, who's been known to wear some head-turning ensembles, including ankle-breaking platforms to naked dresses, so when she ditched her bottoms for a meeting in Los Angeles on Thursday, everyone paid attention.

The multi-hyphenate was spotted arriving at the Four Seasons Hotel in Beverly Hills wearing an oversized black buttoned blazer with a shiny houndstooth print layered over a coordinating turtleneck. Black slouchy knee-high stiletto boots and a matching Birkin bag complemented the cinched minidress, and she accessorized with diamond studs and black shield sunglasses.

Lopez's caramel hair was blown out to perfection and parted down the middle, and she kept her glam very simple, opting for a bronzed complexion and glossy lip.

This all-black sophisticated and trendy look (I mean, three-in-one: blazer, no pants, slouchy boots) just further proves that J.Lo gets better with age. The singer and actress, who is 54, recently spoke with Elle for its 2023 Women in Hollywood issue, and she quite agrees that she ages like fine wine — actually just that all women get "sexier" the older they get.

“As you get older and you have more experience, you become a richer human being, and you have more to offer,” she told the publication. “As you get older and you have more experience, you become a richer human being, and you have more to offer."

“People have realized that women just get sexier as they get older," she continued. "They get more learned and more rich with character. All of that is very beautiful and attractive, and not just physically, but on the inside, the beauty that you gain as you get older, the wisdom you gain.”
