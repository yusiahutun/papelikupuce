Liver King’s Biography

Brian Johnson, better known as Liver King, was born in the United States and raised by his mother in San Antonio after his father passed away when he was young. Despite not getting to know him, Brian’s father had a lasting impact on who he became and shaped the person that is now known as Liver King.

Contrary to what many people believed, King was not a tough child; rather, he was quite small and often the target of bullying at school. In one instance, his tormentors even took away his shoes and forced him to walk home barefoot.

After completing his high school education, he initially decided to pursue a medical degree at Texas Tech University. Unfortunately, during orientation, he chose to drop out and take up employment with a pharmaceutical company instead. Fortunately though, according to the information on his website, it appears that he eventually returned back to university and successfully graduated from the program.
