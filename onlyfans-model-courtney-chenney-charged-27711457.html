OnlyFans model Courtney Chenney charged with stabbing her boyfriend in apartment

OnlyFans star Courtney Clenney has been charged with the fatal stabbing of her boyfriend earlier this year.

Clenney, 25, known as Courtney Tailor on her social media platforms, was arrested in Hawaii on Wednesday and is currently awaiting extradition to Florida.

The social media influencer will face a second degree murder charge for the alleged murder of her estranged boyfriend, 27-year-old Christian Obumseli, in April.

Prieto claimed that Clenney was forced to stab Obumseli after he grabbed her by the throat following weeks of stalking the OnlyFans star.
