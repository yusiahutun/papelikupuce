Baby Socks Needed! Lauren Parsekian and Husband Are Pregnant With First Baby

TrendingBy LRU Writer | On: September 22, 2017

The gorgeous Lauren Parsekian is going to be a mom soon as she and her husband Aaron Paul announced the news about her pregnancy via Instagram on Tuesday, September 19.

The Kind Campaign co-founder, Lauren, first announced the news about her pregnancy in a very creative way. She made a collage of baby Lauren, baby Aaron, and a sonogram and shared it on Instagram with a caption, “Oh hi.”

Aaron then shared the picture of his pregnant wife Lauren on his verified Instagram account where he revealed how excited he is as the little ones are all set to enter their lives. He captioned the image as,

"Hey everyone. Look what I did. Words can't express how excited I am that this little one has entered our lives. Just thought it was time I would share this beautiful news with all of you. ??????????."

The excited couple, who are all set to welcome their first child together, got married on May 26, 2013. They exchanged vows in a wedding ceremony in front of their family and friends in Malibu at the Cottage Pavilion at Calamigos Ranch and had a carnival-themed wedding reception.

The pair first met at the Coachella music festival and then got engaged in 2012. Talking about the engagement to PEOPLE in October of 2012, Paul said,

“We got engaged over New Year’s and I walked from the Eiffel Tower to St. Germain yesterday and I went to the stoop, the little step where I proposed to Lauren – and I took a picture of it and I sent it to her.”

The Breaking Bad actor Paul, who enjoys the net worth of $16 million, also took Twitter to hint his engagement.
