A young woman and her recently-retired mother were found Thursday evening viciously beaten to death in their Bronx apartment.

Ida Randolph, 65, and daughter Jana Randolph, 23, were found bound with duct tape and with signs of severe trauma. Their bodies were initially discovered by a relative who stopped by after not hearing from them for days, according to reports.

The pair’s heartbroken family took to Facebook to vent their shared grief over the loss of two of their own while investigators set about establishing suspects and a motive.

'They didn't deserve this': Ida Richardson, 65, and Jana Richardson, 23, were found bound with duct tape and savagely beaten to death in their Bronx apartment

Mother and daughter were found by cops in separate bedrooms, bound with duct tape and covered in bruises, a police spokesperson told MailOnline. 

Police discovered the bodies after receiving a 911 call from an individual claiming to be the son and brother of the dead women, according to NBC New York. He learned off their deaths after breaking into their apartment via the fire escape, a neighbor told the station.

‘The guy came in, go by the fire escape and went to the room,’ Fatima Diallo told NBC New York. 'One minute later, he came out, 'Oh my God, I just saw my sister laying down, I think she is dead.’

Emergency responders made the grisly discovery after breaking down the apartment’s front door, it is not clear how long the two women were dead.

Such a tragedy: Jana and Ida Randolph died a violent death that at least one family member blames on her ex-boyfriend

Investigators are questioning Jana Randolph’s boyfriend in the double murder, according to NBC New York.

The young man had lived with the pair until she recently broke up with him, a neighbor told the New York Daily News.

An NYPD spokesperson declined comment, saying ‘I don’t know of anyone we are questioning,’ when asked if any suspects or leads had been identified.

Randy Hill, Jana Randolph’s uncle, appeared to be convinced the boyfriend did it, even venting his anger on Facebook.

Explaining that he’s now lost two nieces and an aunt to ‘drug dealing wannabe ‘the man’ guys,’ Mr Hill pleaded with ‘every young lady out there’ to ‘wake up and realize that you can do better.’

Cousin Ebony Randolph was overcome with grief.

‘In tears still… it saddens me so much to know that my grandma and cousin was murder,’ the grieving woman wrote on Facebook.

‘I hope they find whoever did this no one deserves this,’ she wrote before writing ‘Wish I was with my dad right now  — feeling sick,’ in a subsequent post.

Just-retired: Ida Randolph should have comfortably lived out her golden years, she instead died a grisly death alongside her beloved daughter

Another cousin was devastated over the loss of his cherished Jana.

‘I am absolutely heart broken now..Cause I have no one to be different with,’ Marc Scott wrote in a Facebook post.

‘Jana thank you for reminding me of everything I've done & how great of a person I can be because you were doing the very same,’ he continued.

Other family members posted photos of the two slain women to social media, but refrained from commenting.

Ida Randolph appears to have just celebrated her retirement, based on pictures shown on Jana Randolph’s Facebook page. 

Instead of enjoying her golden years surrounded by loved ones, Ida Randolph was savagely beaten to death alongside her beloved daughter.
