6 most macho luxury watches of 2022: from IWCs Top Gun pilots timepiece to Breitlings Catha

Mechanical and functional timepieces have followed and supported us through history to the ends of the earth and the depths of the sea. They have followed us as we broke records in speed, set records above water and in the air, and followed us as we soared ever higher, even into space.

Though technology has caught up with the times and watches are no longer the sole piece of precision equipment used to break such records, maisons have found ever more creative ways to remind us of just how crucial a precise functional watch can be. Here are our team’s picks for the best watches in 2022 to remind us of our adventurous spirits and the edges of human achievement that watches have seen us towards.

6 most classic and timeless watch designs from 2022, from Breitling to Tudor

1. IWC Pilot’s Watch Chronograph Top Gun Edition Woodland

IWC celebrates the cultural success (and long overdue return) of Top Gun with a series of pilot chronographs that are built to withstand the worst of the elements – through and through the maverick’s timepiece.

2. Panerai X Razer Luminor Quaranta Special Edition

Watches have helped us explore the ends of the earth – now Panerai and gaming technology company Razer collaborate to do their bit to preserve it with this sustainably built piece.

3. Glashütte Original Seaq Chronograph

Underplaying overt references to the sea, this deep blue chronograph from Glashütte Original comes with a synthetic strap and peacefully evokes calming ocean depths.

4. Ulysse Nardin Freak S Limited Edition

Depicting an intricately engineered craft set against the night sky, this piece from the Freak series is dedicated to space travel and heavily evokes a sense of cosmic wanderlust.

5. Breitling Navitimer B01 Cathay Pacific Limited Edition

Breitling celebrates the 70th anniversary of its Navitimer by partnering with Cathay to produce an elegant watch fit to take to the skies, with a dial washed in the airline’s signature shade of blue.

6. Omega Speedmaster X-33 Marstimer

Omega takes pilot watch functions to another world as the Marstimer tracks both Earth and Mars time. The digital display glows against the pitch black dial, evoking deep space as you speed on to the fourth planet from the sun.
