Have you ever heard about a female yacht driver?

If not, then you are about to know one.

Among the handful of female yatch driver, Captain Sandy Yawn, who has more than 28 years of experience in yachting, became the first female yachter to star in the Bravo's reality series, Below Deck: Mediterranean, which opened the gateway to the popularity.

Not only that, but Sandy's low-key personal life has also attracted a lot of audiences wanting to know more about her married life.

If you too remain curious to get acquainted with her married life, then this article's just for you!

Dating; Qualities Of Spouse

The successful yatch driver, Sandy Yawn was all about maintaining her personal life very low-key. Well, she has already crossed the marriageable age, and it would be hard for her followers to know that she has not bound to any relationship till date. But she once hinted about her gay relationship.

Revealing that she had a girlfriend, she told Vital VOICE magazine in May 2017, 

"I'm female, I happen to have a girlfriend or whatever."

Even though the 53-year-old proud driver did come out with her sexual preference, she has not revealed her partner's identity and did not mention if she got married to her or just ended the relationship without escalation.

But the major bite of the Los Angeles resident's dating life came on May 2018 while talking to Bravo TV. Revealing she was involved in a low-key long-distance dating relationship, she stated,  

"You know, I have dated in L.A., and it was great so far. But the minute I went to Europe, it changed, it shifted. I believe that distance does not make the heart grow fonder; I think it just makes you forget. And they get jealous, and you can't talk because you're busy running the boat, and you're at sea. The cell phone reception is bad, and then on the flip side is when you feel lonely, and you want to talk to your person, and they're busy."

Also, the world renowned captain added that she has never dated anyone from the yachting industry for the situation can become too "sticky." As put in Sandy's words, "it's hard to have a relationship being a yacht captain." She explained,

"And most of my friends that are yacht captains, they've divorced or they hook up with people in the industry, and so far I haven't met anyone, really. It's about the person for me."

Sandy has participated in the show, Below Deck Mediterranean, where every season is filled with many flirtings, hooking up and breakups. However, she has remained stays far from such issues.
