20 Times Jason Momoa and Lisa Bonet Made Coordinating Outfits Cool

We have to admit we were bummed when Jason Momoa and Lisa Bonet announced in a joint statement they were"parting ways in marriage" due to "seismic shifts" in their relationship. After all, their love story is fascinating, their family dynamic is inspiring (Momoa and Bonet's ex, Lenny Kravitz, are pals!), and their synchronized style is pretty darn cute, too. Not only have these two been known to wear his-and-her suits on the red carpet, but they also share a very similar aesthetic and have been dressing alike for years. But alas, it wasn't meant to be.

Even if their relationship is over, that doesn't mean we can't look back fondly at some of our favorite coordinating outfits throughout their 16-year relationship. Since their first red carpet appearance, it has been obvious this duo is in fashion harmony. Both Momoa and Bonet seem to enjoy scarves and jewelry, have a thing for leather jackets, and give off a relaxed, chill, eccentric sort of vibe — in the coolest and best way possible.

They also didn't shy away from matching with Momoa's favorite rose-colored hue. "Pink is just a beautiful color," he told InStyle. "And I'm pretty secure in my masculinity. I don't really give a shit what anyone thinks."

When It Was All in the Details

In addition to the black jackets and coordinating pants, take a closer look and you'll see how both stars worked some orange-red jewelry into their outfits back in 2010.

When They Each Needed Something Extra

Such as two skinny scarves, to add a pop of pattern. Also in sync? Their smoldering stares.

When It Was a Full Family Affair

Along with Bonet's daughter, Zoë Kravitz, the couple chose dark, structured, form-fitting outfits for the premiere of Conan The Barbarian.

When There Was a Classic Color Scheme

You really can't go wrong with black and white, as seen here on the couple at the 2011 HBO Emmy Awards. Momoa kept it classic with a black suit and white dress shirt, while Bonet looked gorgeous in a white gown with a sheer black overlay.

When They Both Had Fun With Their Looks

This couple isn't scared to take some fashion risks. In fact, with the ruffle collar on Bonet's shirt and the polka dots on Momoa's scarf, they may have been the inspiration behind the clown trend.

When the Couple Made Suits Seem Casual

They dressed them down with a T-shirt, distressed boots, and an old hat.

When They Both Had the Same Idea

AKA layering with a leather jacket. They also used scarves to add a pop of color. Bonet tied a blue scarf around her neck, while Momoa tucked a black patterned scarf into his pocket.

When They Stepped Out in Dark Colors and Black Boots

You can't go wrong in coordinating all-black outfits and boots. Momoa kept his look rugged in worn-in boots, while Bonet opted for black booties.

When They Couldn't Pass Up a Theme

Once you realize this is the Mad Max: Fury Road premiere, these outfits make total sense. Momoa accented his look with a patterned shawl slung over his shoulder, while Bonet opted for a furry shawl.

When They Found the Happy Medium Between Fancy and Casual

Between Momoa's suit and T-shirt pairing and Bonet's flowy back dress, the couple looked sleek and comfy at the same time at the 2015 InStyle Awards.

When Outerwear Made Their Outfits

Plus, Bonet made sure to include her number one fashion staple: a hat.

When They Looked Ready to Party

At the Justice League premiere, the two strayed from their typical loose, laid-back vibe, going with a tailored navy suit and a tight red dress.

When Velvet Was the Common Thread

Bonet made her printed dress seem less formal with a belt and headscarf. Momoa, on the other hand, paired his blazer with gray leather pants.

When It Was All About the Length

For the Aquaman premiere, the couple brought their A-game. Not only did Momoa trade his blazer for a top coat, but the fringe detail on Bonet's Alexander McQueen dress grazed the floor.

When the Color of the Night Was Pink

Wrist scrunchie and all! The 2019 Academy Awards was all about his favorite hue.

...Even at the After Party

The duo traded their red carpet ensembles for ones in a slightly darker shade.

When Momoa's Jacket Matched Bonet's Dress

That pop of pink didn't just look good on him — it coordinated with her printed dress, too.

When the Fendi Fashion Show Called For Sheer

He saw her naked dress and raised her a naked shirt.

When Things Were Sweet and Subtle

Like how the pattern on Bonet's white, purple, and pink number paired perfectly with Momoa's purple suit. Let's not overlook how her black heart-shaped purse also coordinates with his black shirt.

When They Actually Wore the Same Thing

Their his-and-her suits stole all our attention at the 2019 premiere of Apple TV+'s See. They accented their looks with eye-catching necklaces, which Momoa said on his Instagram story was a "very special gift from my wife."
