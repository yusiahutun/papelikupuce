Look 1: Beach to Cocktails

Media Platforms Design Team

Describe your style in 3 words. Relaxed, tomboy, retro Does living in LA influence your style? Yes. I go on a lot of road trips up and down the coast, where I'm living out of the car and all I've packed is a backpack of clothes. So, that allows me to keep my style really simple and flexible—bringing just a pair of pants, a pair of shorts, a couple shirts and a jacket. I also skateboard a lot so that may be why I'm such a tomboy. You'll mostly see me kicking around in something super relaxed. Your style icons, one living, one dead? Jane Birkin and Nico from the Velvet Underground

If you could only wear one designer for the rest of your life, who would it be? Chloé If you could only wear one piece of jewelry, what would it be? I have a vintage turquoise and silver ring that has my zodiac sign (aquarius) embellished on it that my man gave me a few years back. First fashion memory? When I had my first ever shoot­ it was with Italian Vogue at age 16 and I was zipped into this insanely tight velvet black gown, wore a gas mask, and was handling a fake plastic spray painted metallic machine gun. The photographer was Steven Klein.

Bonus Look: Casting Day

Media Platforms Design TeamWhat is your evening style—suiting or a cocktail dress? I think a mini skirt, black leather boots, and a relaxed vintage tee best describes what I'd wear for night. How often will you wear a favorite piece? I don't really have any rules. I would never intentionally wear the same exact outfit out two nights in a row....but then again, I don't think I'd care if I got caught. I wear whatever I want when I feel like it, as long as I feel good in it. Are you an editor or builder—Do you clean out your closets at the end of each season? How often do you purge your wardrobe? I would call myself a collector. I go vintage shopping a lot, whether it's at my local flea market or eBay. I often get tired of things and will clean out my closet as soon as it starts to overflow. Usually cleaning out my closet means putting everything in a box and into storage until I go through it again and decide I can't actually give it up. I am building an etsy store though, that should help. Photos by Nico Guilis of Find Your California; Hair and Makeup by Dee Daly.

Kerry Pieri is Harper's Bazaar's Digital Fashion/Features Director. Kerry oversees the fashion vertical for BAZAAR.com, including launching digital covers, styling celebrity features, and managing recurring features including Brand Watch and My Life in Three Looks. In addition, Kerry edits the “best of” each season, from shoes to bags, denim and coats, and is consistently looking for shifts in the fashion realm to lend a voice. Kerry formerly served as the Editorial Director of  StyleCaster.com and as a producer at Full Frontal Fashion. She lives in New York with her husband Steve, and baby girl Lila Sky.
