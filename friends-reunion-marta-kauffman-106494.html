Friends Co-Creator Marta Kauffman Says a Reunion Show Will Never Happen Find Out Why

Well, this is depressing.

In a revealing new interview, Friends co-creator Marta Kauffman sadly confirmed a reunion show starring Jennifer Aniston (as Rachel Green), Courteney Cox (as Monica Geller), Lisa Kudrow (as Phoebe Buffay), Matthew Perry (as Chandler Bing), David Schwimmer (as Ross Geller) and Matt LeBlanc (as Joey Tribbiani) will “never happen.”

The cast of Friends.

“[Fans still] nag me all the time about it, and my answer is that Friends was a show about that time in your life when your friends are your family, and in a weird way, this isn’t that different. Once you start having family of your own it changes, and the show was over,” the 59-year-old TV producer explained.

“[The show] has a lifespan like anything else, and there’s no reason to continue doing it just because people miss the characters,” Marta told Deadline. “Watch the old ones; there is no way we could win that. And there’s no way it would be satisfying and it’ll never happen. We’ll never do it.”
