Rachel Stuhlmann - Age, Family, Bio

Social media influencer, tennis player and journalist who was named the world's No. 1 tennis influencer by sports blog Outkick. She works for Top Court, a tennis platform, where she writes blog posts and acts as media manager. She has amassed over 310,000 followers on her rstuhlmann Instagram account, where she posts pictures from tennis tournaments around the world.

Before Fame

She played tennis in high school and was nationally ranked on the U.S. Tennis Association's Junior Circuit. She later went on to play Division I collegiate tennis at the University of Missouri, where she majored in nutrition and fitness.

Trivia

She is also active on her rachelstuhlmann TikTok account, where she has 50,000 followers and over 380,000 likes. She often posts updates about the tennis world and clips of herself playing tennis. She posted a video in August 2022 where she shares her latest tennis photoshoot. It received over 83,000 views.

Family Life

She was born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri. Her parents are Doug and Lisa Stuhlmann.

Associated With

She interviewed Andrey Rublev for Taste of Tennis in September 2022.
