Sunny Anderson's Short Bio:

Sunny Anderson was born on 9th April 1975 in  Lawton, Oklahoma but grew to be an Army Brat. She did her schooling from Madison High School in San Antonio, Texas and after graduation, she joined the United Stated Airforce. 

Sunny career boosted when she came into limelight after she was given an opportunity to host the show “Cooking For Real” on the Food Network. At the age of 41, she has appeared on shows like Good Morning America, The Early Show, The View and The Talk as a guest chef. 

She has an estimated net worth of $5million dollar. In her website, she shares her food recipes and has even published her cookbook Sunny’s Kitchen: Easy Food for Real Life in the year 2013.
