Where is Armie Hammer now?

According to Page Six, as of early 2024, the American actor ended his engagement and relationship with former fiancee Marina Gris. The ex-couple reportedly began dating in July 2023 before getting engaged in October 2023.

He also made a return to social media, first posting a puzzling train clip on his once-wiped Instagram feed on 1 November 2023. He has since gone on to upload twice more (at the time of writing).

What are the allegations against Hammer?

According to People, on 12 January 2021, an Instagram account named House of Effie anonymously shared sexual assault allegations against the actor. The allegations included text messages allegedly sent by him centred around sensual sexual practices and cannibalistic fantasies.

Two days later, Hammer's ex-girlfriend Courtney Vucekovich came forward. She alleged that the actor had strange fantasies when they dated, terming him as a wannabe Hannibal Lecter.
