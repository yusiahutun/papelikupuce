Erika Christensen posts topless throwback snap breastfeeding daughter

Erika Christensen posted a topless throwback snap in honor of Mother's Day.

The actress shared a breastfeeding photo of herself and then newborn daughter Shane - now two - to pay tribute to all hardworking mothers, including her own. 

'Today I definitely focused on my gratitude for my mom, who did and does so much to teach me not THAT the seemingly impossible is possible, but HOW.' she captioned the intimate picture.

Throwback: Erika Christensen posted a topless throwback snap in honor of Mother's Day

'A private moment from the beginning of this relationship that’s still in its beginnings,' began the caption in reference to her baby girl.

Then the Parenthood actress continued to wax on about the positive influence her mother possessed.

'The only time I actually cried was after I had already hired the lactation consultant, and she made it seem so easy. 

'Then she left and I tried on my own and I still couldn’t figure it out,” she said in October,' she told People in October.

Erika and her husband Cole Maness welcomed Shane in June 2016.
