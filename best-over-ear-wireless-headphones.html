Unlike the wired counterparts, over-ear wireless headphones save you from the headache of dealing with tangling cables. They also allow you to move around conveniently as they connect via Bluetooth.

If you’re looking for over-ear wireless headphones to take your listening experience to the next level, consider the battery life, availability of smart features, sound quality, durability, weight, and comfort.

Here are the best over-ear wireless headphones you’ll find on the market today.

FAQ

Q: Are Over-Ear Wireless Headphones Repairable?

There are common issues you’re likely to encounter when using wireless headphones, but most are fixable.

Sometimes, the headphones may fail to pair with audio devices, face interference from time to time, generate a buzzing sound, or fail to charge. Depending on the problem, you can bring your headphones to life in minutes in the comfort of your home.

But if the problem is beyond your DIY skills, visit a professional repair store. If the headphones are old, consider replacing them to enjoy new features.

Q: Can I Game With Over-Ear Wireless Headphones?

Yes, you can. Over-ear headphones offer full-ear coverage, meaning the noise isolation capabilities are better than on-ear models. You hear realistic and more prominent audio when gaming, thus boosting concentration when hunting down opponents.

However, when buying wireless headphones, consider picking a pair with low latency mode to eliminate audio lag that may cost you a win.

Q: Can I Use Over-Ear Wireless Headphones on Planes?

Yes. Most connect to your devices via short-range Bluetooth, so they have zero to minimal effects on the aircraft. But remember to check if the airline you plan to use permits the use of wireless headphones.

In addition, most models have epic battery life even with ANC on, making them ideal for long flights.
