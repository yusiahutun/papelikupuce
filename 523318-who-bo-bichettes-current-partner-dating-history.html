Early childhood 

Joseph Bo Bichette was born on 5th March 1998 in Orlando, Florida, to Dante and Mariana Bichette. He is an American national with Brazilian heritage and has a brother, Dante Bichette.

Although initially homeschooled, he played for Lakewood High School in St. Petersburg, Florida, USA, before transitioning to Arizona State University. He played for the Arizona State Sun Devils in college Baseball at the institution.

His parents' decision to name him Bo was influenced by his father, Dante, being a fan of the multi-sport athlete Bo Jackson. The name was unique, like a nickname, and stuck amongst his peers.

Career

The Toronto Blue Jays picked Bo Bichette as the 66th overall pick in the second round of the 2016 Major League Baseball draft. Bo's mind was set on joining the Toronto Blues after rejecting four offers from other clubs during the draft to sign with the team for a $1.1 million bonus transfer fee.
