Ella Emhoff Biography, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth

Ella Emhoff is an American artist, prominently known as the step-daughter of Kamala Harris, the 49th vice president of the United States. She is currently studying apparel and textiles at Parsons School of Design.

Early Life

Ella Rose Emhoff was born on May 29, 1999, in the United States. She was born to a father Doug Emhoff and mother Kerstin Emhoff. She has a brother named Cole Emhoff who is Her father is a lawyer and partner at DLA Piper since 2017 and her mother Kerstin is a film producer and CEO of Prettybird. In 2008, her parents got divorced and her father Doug married Kamala Harris on August 22, 2014. Kamala is now the stepmother of Ella and Cole. Ella went to a private high school in Los Angeles where she was also on the swim team, she graduated in 2018. Currently, she is pursuing her Bachelor of Fine Arts in apparel and textiles at Parsons School of Design, The New School in New York City.
