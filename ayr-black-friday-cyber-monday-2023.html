The Best AYR Black Friday Sale 2023 Deals to Shop

When it comes to Black Friday fashion deals that warrant minimizing all other tabs, AYR’s sits at the top of our lists. The cult-favorite brand, whose fans include Oprah, legions of models, and all of Bazaar, is taking 50 percent off select looks now through November 27. Take note: This is the steepest Black Friday discount AYR has ever offered.

Overflowing with elevated everyday pieces that shoppers can expect to wear weekly—in essence, the classics AYR has become so beloved for—AYR's Black Friday sale includes finds as reasonably priced as $20. Highlights not to be missed include a fresh crop of denim styles, such as their extremely popular Secret Sauce jeans and their classic POP straight-leg ankle jeans in a wear-with-everything wash.

Those whose wardrobes are lacking reliable tops are about to get a major closet upgrade as well. AYR's Black Friday sale is dripping in deals on everything from Breton striped T-shirts to the brand's famous button-downs. This even includes a striped version of the label's Deep End top, which was famously included as one of Oprah's Favorite Things back in 2020. Ms. Winfrey is hardly this best-seller's only fan, by the way: Bazaar decreed the shirt the only button-down worth needing in 2021.

AYR's most devoted disciples will recall that the brand's 2022 Black Friday deals clocked in at select looks for up to 40 perfect off. Those just tuning in or perhaps dipping their toes into the label for the first time can enjoy this year's wildly steep deals all the same. Just take care to add your most needed basics to cart quickly. These deals are final sale and here for a good time, not a long time—the sale ends on Cyber Monday.
