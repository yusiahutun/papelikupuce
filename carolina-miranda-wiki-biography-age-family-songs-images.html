Carolina Miranda: Career

Quickly after she registered herself in an acting class, she was blotched by a director who invited her for the castings at Azteca, the second-highest mass media company in Mexico.
In 2012, she made her debut on Mexican TV with the daily soap theatre “Los Rey” in which she Portrayed the character of Delfina Rey Ortuña “Fina.”

In 2014, she portrayed the character of Carmen Bravo Diaz in the Mexican telenovela “Las Bravo.”
In December 2016, she came into the spotlight with the American telenovela “Señora Acero” (Season-3) in which she portrayed the lead actress, Vicenta Acero, a humble and fussed raccoon who contrabanded foreigners across the U.S.–Mexico border.
In 2017, the fourth season of “Señora Acero” became the popular Spanish program in its time schedule.

Web series

She made her digital debut in 2019 when she portrayed the lead character of Clara Díaz in the Mexican comedy show “Claramente.” The plot of the show revolves around Clara, a journalist with an exceptional power to read the minds of men.
In 2021, she achieved international acknowledgement with Netflix’s Mexican thriller series, “Who Killed Sara? in which she portrayed the character of Elisa, the youngest of the Lazcano siblings who after coming from her studies finds out that her family is indicted of murder.

Carolina Miranda: Awards and achievements

In 2017, she won PRODU Award in the nomination of debut Actress, Series, Superseries, or Telenovela of the Year for the television series “Señora Acero” (2016).


In November 2018, she got Premio Arlequín (Arlequin Award Mexico) for Character Creation and Development.
In December 2019, she got Micrófono de Oro (Golden Microphone) in the nomination of Best Actress.
