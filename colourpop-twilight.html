ColourPop's 'Twilight' Collaboration Is Finally Back in Stock

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

Where were you when you first heard the iconic line, “You better hold on tight, spider monkey”? Edward Cullen’s cheesy but cute question is only one of many reasons the Twilight movies are so iconic. Even a whopping 15 years after the first film was released, the Twilight fever hasn’t diminished, much to Robert Pattinson and Kristen Stewart’s dismay. To wit, ColourPop rolled out its Twilight collaboration in January, and it’s finally back in stock.

It’s been 15 years. It’s long past spooky season. But none of that mattered when ColourPop teased its collection. All the brand posted was a blood-red lip gloss with a large drip against a moody background. Fans lost their minds, writing, “Besties….. if this is twilight……,” and “I stg if this is a twilight collection I will pass away” and “THE TREES IN THE BACKGROUND???? FORKS??? TWILIGHT COLLECTION???” Yup.

On January 11, the affordable beauty brand launched its full collection inspired by Twilight. We’re talking about a diamond skin-inspired Shimmering Dry Body Oil and vampy red Lux Lip Oil. The Twilight Eyeshadow Palette has the cutest shade names (Ice Cold, Impossibly Fast, Seventeen) but sadly, “Loca” is nowhere to be found.

If you weren’t quick enough to check out in January, ColourPop is restocking the collection on February 29 at 1 p.m. ET. If the initial launch is any indication of how the restock will go, we highly recommend you enter your card info before fans sell out the collection again. Most products are only $10 or $11, so get ready to load up your cart!

  • Twilight Eyeshadow Palette

    “So the lion fell in love with the lamb…” and 15 shades in Matte, Metallic, Matte Sparkle, and Pearlescent Glitter finishes.

  • SOL Shimmering Dry Body Oil

    The new Like Diamonds SOL Shimmering Dry Body Oil comes in a multidimensional glow like Edward’s skin in the sun.

  • Lux Lip Oil

    There are three new Lux Lip Oils to choose from: Bells (a vampy blackened red), Team Edward (a deep rich blue with silver pinpoints), and Team Jacob (a sheer black with silver pinpoints).

  • Super Shock Highlighter

    The two new Super Shock Highlighters come in Vampire Skin (an icy white with sparkling multi-dimensional pinpoints) and Meadow (an opalescent duo-chrome lavender with multi-dimensional pinpoints).

  • Graphix Ink Liner

    The ultra-pigmented Graphix Ink Liner comes in: Vampire (a metallic red), Coven (a rich black with sparkling silver pinpoints), and Forever (a metallic forest green with green pinpoints).
