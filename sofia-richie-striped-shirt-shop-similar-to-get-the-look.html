Sofia Richies 'quiet luxury' shirt is all you need to add to your wardrobe this summer

The Gen Z style icon wore a stunning satin shirt

    Right now it’s Sofia Richie Grainge’s world and we’re just living in it. Suffice to say our obsession with the 24-year-old’s sartorial agenda is in line with the rest of the planet. Ever since her star-studded wedding in the South of France which consisted of: the ultimate quiet luxury pre-nuptial looks; a trio of ultra chic Chanel wedding gowns and most elite honeymoon wardrobe, the latest It-girl on the scene is currently the only name on fashion fans' lips.

    Anytime she uploads an Instagram post or a TikTok (kudos to her for giving us behind the scenes 'GRWM' videos of pretty much everything), her fans are desperate to know exactly where her outfits are from. FYI, we include ourselves in said fan base.
