Last-Minute Halloween Costumes That Are Easy and Fun

Between shuttling your kids to practice, all those zoom meetings, and having to find the perfect Halloween costumes for your little ones, the search for your Halloween costume has fallen by the wayside. We get it: You’re busy — but you may still want to dress up for Halloween.  Lucky for you, there’s no need to sacrifice Halloween festivities just to keep up with your hectic schedule. Let these DIY last-minute Halloween costumes rescue your evening!

From spooky to sweet, these costumes take no time at all but will still give you a chance at winning your the best-costume award. Whether you’re looking for sweet Halloween costumes, like a cute Morton Salt girl costume, or an edgier ventriloquist doll Halloween costume, we’ve got you covered.

From sweet and adorable to funny and fabulous, you’ll find plenty of options in this collection of last-minute Halloween costumes even if you waited until days before. And remember above all else to have fun when you head out there!
