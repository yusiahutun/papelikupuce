1

Arrowhead Pine Rose: Twin Peaks, CA

Paige Jones

With weddings to suit parties of 20 to 175, this cabin filled retreat feels less like a classic 'resort' due to it's authentic woodland feel. Whether you book an intimate ceremony amongst the evergreens or a larger reception overlooking Lake Arrowhead, take advantage of the on-site lodging for a wedding weekend. While this site may need a designer's touch when it comes to florals, decor and choosing the right dress for the setting, it's natural background will undoubtedly serve up that rustic vibe you've been eyeing–barn not included.

2

Casa Violeta: Tulum, Mexico

Kerry Beach Events

This dreamy bohemian beach venue gives "Vamos a la Playa" a whole new meaning. Bordered by the white sand beaches of Tulum, this hotel merges Caribbean style eats, daily yoga and a healing spa for a wedding weekend filled with relaxation. The stark white aesthetic and elegant table spreads makes for an opulent desert-island feel that you'll never want to escape from.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

3

Camp Navarro: Philo, CA

Tyler Branch

Deep within the Redwood, CA grounds, Camp Navarro lies in the wine country's hidden coastal valley. The 200+ acre campground is the chic summer camp you never had, complete with luxe vacation treats and wifi. Day activities like archery and rock climbing will enthrall sports enthusiasts, while rustic detail will satisfy any nature lover.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

4

Maderas Village: Rivas, Nicaragua

Maderas Village

Heaven takes physical form at the Maderas Village resort on a hillside beach in Nicaragua. From surf sessions to an intimate ocean-side ceremony, this venue is ideal for for active wave-seekers. This jungle retreat includes a yoga studio, a screening area and a meditation room, as well as an energy-healing spa and on-site surfing instruction.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

5

Peacock Pavilions: Marrakesh, Morroco

Peacock Pavillions

Think billowing tents, colorful lanterns, eclectic carpets and floating candles galore. Peacock Pavilions is noted for it's chic, colorful receptions, tailored Moroccan menus, traditional henna and pool parties to cap off the night's festivities. Special performances are also available to make your day feel that much more authentic, with belly dancers, fire breathers camel rides and more available for bookings.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

6

The Barn In Tivoli: Tivoli, NY

Cargo Collective

Barn weddings have been all over Pinterest for years, but for the couple who just isn't over that rustic charm, The Barn In Tivoli in upstate New York has the look and laid-back feel you've been eyeing. Clad with 4.5 acres of lush greenery, and a yoga deck and fire, this is the perfect wedding getaway for an elopement with a hand-crafted, farm-to-table feel.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

Moab Under Canvas: Moab, UT

Studio Castillero

Spending a night under the stars has never been so easy and large-party friendly. Moab Under Canvas's campground is exquisite for experiencing the outdoors without losing the luxe amenities of a five-star hotel. Located in Moab, Yellowstone & Glacier Park, MT, this spot delivers your dream tipis to your ceremony site, with the option to purchase a permit to have bell tents brought in with décor– all designed and organized to your specifications. Dine communally with your family and spend the night glamping for a low-key wedding your guests will never forget.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

8

Treehouse Point: Issaquah, WA

Jagger Photography

If you're an adrenaline junkie meets nature lover, take your need for elevation to the next level (literally) with a tree-top wedding at Treehouse Point. This Washington state forest venue features modern in-air dwellings that look over lush foliage, cascading waterfalls and dreamy forest air. The venue offers different tree houses to accommodate small parties or large in sky-high fashion.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

9

The Ruin Venue: Pioneertown, CA

Caleb John Hill

Joshua Tree isn't just for camping (or glamping). This gem of a venue embraces the natural beauty of the desert, offering a blank canvas to make your wedding distinctively yours. This site's peaceful vibes and outstanding weather offer up the cool factor of Coachella, without the pomp and circumstance of a Palm Springs celebration. In this easy-chic atmosphere, think less mid-century modern and more Baja-inspired textiles paired with wildflower arrangements.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

Dunton Hot Springs: Dolores, CO

Michele Hart

Nestled in a tiny town in the Colorado Rockies, Dunton Hot Springs is laced with cozy cabins, scenic trails and beautiful hot springs all year round. After an easy-going ceremony taking in the scenic views, visit their seasoned Saloon for your after party, which feels like a true time-warp back to the Colorado mining days.
