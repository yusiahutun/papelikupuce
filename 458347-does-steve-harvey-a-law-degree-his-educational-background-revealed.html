Does Steve Harvey have a law degree?

The simple answer is no. Steve Harvey doesn't possess a law degree. The author and television host never went to law school.

Any person authorised to act as a judge in a real-world court must have fulfilled all educational and experience requirements that fit the role. In this case, one must have earned their bachelor’s degree and taken the LSAT (Law School Admissions Test).

Once admitted into law school and completing all the education modules, the student graduates with a Juris Doctor degree. They are also required to pass their bar exam. After earning their education, the person can start practising and earn experience as a lawyer in the courtroom.

You can apply to become a judge only after getting the proper exposure in the courtroom. In addition, you must have practised law for at least two years.
