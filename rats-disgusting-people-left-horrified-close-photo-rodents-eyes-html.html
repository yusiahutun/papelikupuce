Rat's disgusting! People left horrified by close-up photo of a rodent's eyes

Thousands of Reddit users have been horrified after a close-up photo of a pet rat's bulging eyes. 

While some keep the them as pets, most wouldn't dream of welcoming a disease-spreading rodent into their home. 

But a warts-and-all snap of a pet rat that was shared on Reddit has left readers aghast at the rodent's bulging peepers.  

Posting under a Reddit thread, user alexand3rl wrote: 'Today I learned you can see a rat's pupils when light hits at the right angle.'

Reddit users were left horrified when they stumbled upon the bulging eyes of a rat, but others are defending the species 

The picture shows the user's pet rat popping its little head out of its cage during a particularly sunny hour of the day - revealing two swollen-looking eyes. 

In the photo, the creature appears to be staring directly at the camera, though its pupils faced opposite directions. 

Thousands of Reddit users, who seemed fond of the rat, flooded the comments with potential explanations.  

One joked: 'Loosen its necktie before taking next picture.' 

Some defended the species' reputation in general, one saying: 'This photo does NOT help the rat cause at all.'

Another agreed: 'They've been getting several centuries of bad press, frankly.'

While many wouldn't dare to go near a rat, some are keeping them as pets but owners are shocked at how large their eyes are 

Some, however, offered a more scientific contribution, with one user explaining that bulging eyes can be a result of 'bruxing', which is where a rat grinds or chatters its teeth together.

They explained that this can be an expression of endearment, but added: 'Sometimes when they do this they get so into it that their eyes will actually bulge in and out really quickly. 

'It just means they’re super duper happy but man it freaked me out the first time.' 

According to PetMD: 'Bruxing is a soft but repetitive grinding of the incisors, or front teeth.'

The website explained that when a rat is bruxing, it 'can cause the bulbous eyes to pop out of the socket even more.'

So, although somewhat petrifying, your rat may be more comfortable with you if they show bulging eyes. 

The revelation follows another rat-related Reddit post, which showed how easily rats can scurry up a toilet post. 

Users were not as taken with the post which showed a rat making its way up the toilet bowl, one person wrote: 'New fear unlocked.'
