Wedding fragrance services - Perfume consultations for brides

The Salon de Parfums at Harrods features some of the world's finest and rarest scents alongside exclusive bespoke perfume creation and personalisation services. The exquisite fragrance gallery is home to 25 luxury brands, from Tom Ford to Penhaligon's, the latter being known for its signature fragrance profiling, a complimentary service that helps guide you on an olfactory journey of discovery, aiding the selection of a fragrance that best reflects you and your wedding. The British perfumer also offers a semi and fully bespoke programme for custom perfume creation, the ultimate being the Bespoke by Alberto Morillas service. The result, delivered in a luxury chest, contains the fragrance formula which cannot be replicated without your permission, making it truly all yours.

The Roja Dove Haute Perfumerie boutique offers similar luxury, with the perfumer's Legacy collection that, once purchased, can be bought by no one else, though you can reorder it for life. Semi-bespoke and ‘Odour Profiling’ – “like an haute-couture fragrance fitting” – are available too.

In a similar vein, iconic fragrance house Guerlain offers Le Parfum Sur Mesure, a service providing the rare opportunity to have a scent created just for you by its legendary nose Thierry Wasser, creator of La Petite Robe Noire and L’Homme Ideal.

Elsewhere in the Salon de Parfums you’ll find Frédéric Malle's boutique and his collection composed by some of the world's greatest perfumers. Here a luxury service is available to pin-point your signature scent, and one that best reflects you and your wedding – or any special occasion.

Bond No.9 – a brand that pays tribute to New York City – also offers custom-fragrance blending, perfect for anyone searching for something unique. There are three services on offer; a made-from-scratch signature scent, a do-it-yourself demi-custom blend, and a mix-at-home fragrancing system coined Private Bond, which allows you to blend scents suiting your mood.

Salon de Parfums, Harrods, plan your visit to enquire
