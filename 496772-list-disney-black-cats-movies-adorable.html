1. Lucifer - Cinderella

Lucifer, also known as Luci, is a male Disney cat character that acts as a minor antagonist in the Disneys 1950 animated movie Cinderella. He is a black cat owned by the Tremaine family and has a cunning and malicious personality. Lucifer oppresses Cinderella, just like his masters.

His schemes mainly entail trying to kill the mouse at the Château and sabotaging her bloodhound, Bruno. Interestingly, Lucifer is loyal to his master and never compromises. Apart from the Cinderella movie, Lucifer has appeared in several films, including House of Mouse.

2. Figaro - Pinocchio

You cannot imagine black and white Disney cats without thinking of the adorable but cheeky Figaro. Figaro is a sweet Tuxedo cat owned by the generous woodcarver Geppetto. His cute black and white fur attracts most characters to play with him.

He often takes minor roles in most Disney movies like Mickey Mouse Works, House of Mouse, and Mickey's Once Upon a Christmas. Even though his roles are minor, Figaro is a recurring character that many Disney movie lovers are fond of.
