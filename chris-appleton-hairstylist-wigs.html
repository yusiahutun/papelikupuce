Hairstylist Chris Appleton Shows Us His Insane Collection of Celebrity Wigs

With a celebrity client roster that includes Ariana Grande, Kim Kardashian West, Katy Perry, and Jennifer Lopez—Chris Appleton's hair stash is as wild as you'd imagine. We took The Beauty Show on the road to Los Angeles to check out Appleton's massive hair closet and learn a little bit more about the in-demand celebrity hairstylist.

Shop Chris Appleton's Beauty Stash

Appleton, who works with Glam Seamless and Color Wow, has about every style, length, cut, and color of faux hair that you can imagine. Walking through his hair closet is not unlike walking through a museum of celebrity hair, with each piece telling its own story. "There's so much love and prep that goes into the girls—we call them the girls," Appleton says of his stash. Each custom-made wig can cost thousands of dollars, so Appleton and his team spend hours carefully grooming, coloring, and dyeing them. More times than not, you can find him cutting and coloring a weft of hair in a hotel room overnight.

Pick an extension at random in his walk-in beauty closet, and it might just belong to Kardashian West, who decided she wanted pink hair while on a trip to Tokyo. In the corner of the closet is a 50-inch honey-blonde masterpiece—hand-created by Appleton and his team—that Lopez has worn on the red carpet. And in the opposite corner, three candy-colored wigs that Appleton whipped up at the last minute for the KKW Beauty Kimoji Hearts campaign.

Related Story

As a master of hair transformations, Appleton collects wigs not just for fun—but because of the confidence and power it gives his clients. "I fell in love with hair, I remember when I was a kid I enjoyed the idea of making someone look good. It was amazing the transformation you could give someone and the power it kind of gives them of making them look and feel good," Appleton says. "They stand a little bit taller. You create kind of like a character."
