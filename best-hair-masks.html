11 Best Hair Masks for Hydration, Damage Repair, and More

Best for Frizz Control

Alfaparf Milano Semi Di Lino Nutritive Mask

Best for Frizz Control

Alfaparf Milano Semi Di Lino Nutritive Mask

Credit: Amazon
Pros
  • Great for color-treated hair

Townsend considers this nourishing mask pick his absolute favorite. “It turns dry, coarse hair into soft, shiny, manageable hair in just a few minutes,” he says. “I recommend this to all of my clients with dry hair.” The mask uses moisturizing meadowfoam seed oil, along with UV filters, to keep dyed hair from fading.

Best for Color-Treated Hair

Rita Hazan Weekly Remedy Treatment

Best for Color-Treated Hair

Rita Hazan Weekly Remedy Treatment

Credit: Amazon
Pros
  • Safe for keratin-treated hair
Cons
  • Can be used only once a week

This two-step treatment from Beyoncé’s colorist, Rita Hazan, requires a little extra time, but Townsend says that the top-rated mask “penetrates the hair to deliver moisture and proteins, and then seals the cuticle for long-lasting shine and frizz control.” He advises using it once a week for best results.

Best Fortifying Mask

Rossano Ferretti Parma Dolce Nourishing Hair Mask

Best Fortifying Mask

Rossano Ferretti Parma Dolce Nourishing Hair Mask

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Features plumping hyaluronic acid

Spagnuolo notes that Rossano Ferretti Parma’s luxe mask works well to deeply nourish and smooth dry or damaged hair. Its moisturizing formula contains shea butter and keratin to repair existing damage and strengthen hair at the same time.

One satisfied customer writes: “Highly recommended to anyone with dry, brittle hair. This is my problem and it’s hard to control too so getting it into any style is difficult. I’m also getting real value for money from this mask, it’s giving me moisturized, healthy hair with style thrown in. Perfect.” —Frankie888

Best for Bond Building

K18 Leave-In Molecular Repair Hair Mask

Best for Bond Building

K18 Leave-In Molecular Repair Hair Mask

Credit: Amazon
Pros
  • Works quickly

Over 13,000 Amazon customers agree: Beauty brand K18 is a force when it comes to repairing damaged hair. And even better? Each of its bestsellers—including its beloved Leave-In Molecular Repair Hair Mask—take only a few minutes to work. Peptides in the mask go deep into each hair strand to reconnect keratin bonds that may have been broken from chemical treatments.

One satisfied customer writes: “I was a serious skeptic but I bleached the crap outta my hair and it was frizzy beyond belief especially in Florida humidity! Best for Daily Use

Eva NYC Therapy Session Hair Mask

Best for Daily Use

Eva NYC Therapy Session Hair Mask

Credit: Amazon
Pros
  • Easy to apply
  • Lightweight

This argan oil and plant protein mask is light enough to use daily in place of a regular conditioner, or weekly for a more intensive hair treatment. After applying it to wet hair, wait up to five minutes before rinsing out the mask completely.

One satisfied customer writes: “This product is so good! This is my second bottle. My daughter bought it and let me try it and I was hooked! It smells amazing and leaves my hair feeling silky smooth! I use it every time I condition, and leave it on for about 3-5 minutes! Works well with any shampoo I have used! Will definitely continue to buy in the future!” —Anonymous
