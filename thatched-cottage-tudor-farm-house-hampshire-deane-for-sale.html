Thatched Cottage In Hampshire Countryside For Sale

In quintessential thatched cottage style, this charming home set on the edge of the village of Deane fuses traditional character with cosy living. On the market for £850,000, it's storybook architecture at its finest.

Tudor Farm House is ideal for those looking for a less ordinary house: think low ceilings, fireplaces dating from the 1700s, exposed brickwork, rustic beams, and a snug fairy tale feel. It boasts three/four bedrooms, three bathrooms, two reception rooms, and a beautiful mature garden that surrounds the house on three sides.

Kick off your shoes at the door and enter the lively rooms. Off the hallway are the two reception rooms; the dining room and sitting room. Meanwhile, the kitchen lies at the back of the dining room, boasting electric ovens and gas (LPG) hobs. Completing this floor is a downstairs family bathroom, study space (which could also be used as a bedroom) and French doors leading out to the terrace.

Knight Frank

Citrus tones in the upstairs bathroom add warmth and zest, while the two bedrooms stay true to the property's history with wooden beams and casement windows overlooking the glorious garden. There is limited access between the two landing on the first floor through a small passageway with restricted head height.

Tudor Farm House's enclosed and private setting is a real lure for people of all ages. You'll find a terrace off the rear of the house, plus a practical garden shed and summer house. Completing the outside space is a separate vegetable garden with raised beds and a disused greenhouse — ideal if you want to adopt a plot-to-plate lifestyle.

There is separate vehicle access off Manor Farm Lane leading directly into the rear of the garden.

This cottage is on the market for £850,000 with Knight Frank.
