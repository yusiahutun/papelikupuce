2014 Faster Horses Festival Lineup Announced

Summer just got a little hotter with the announcement of the 2014 Faster Horses Festival lineup. Tim McGraw and Miranda Lambert -- and many others -- will be included in the festivities, taking place July 18-20 in Brooklyn, Mich.

This is festival's second year, and although it's a relatively new event, you wouldn't be able to tell by the sizzling scores of stars. Joining McGraw and Lambert are Keith Urban, Little Big Town, Kip Moore, Brett Eldredge, Joe Diffie, Jana Kramer, Ashley Monroe, Parmalee, Cassadee Pope, Dan + Shay, Dee Jay Silver, Chris Stapleton, Cole Swindell and Austin Webb, with more acts to be announced soon.

The Faster Horses Festival had a hugely successful first run last summer, welcoming more than 20,000 fans per day. In addition to the picturesque summertime setting in the Irish Hills of Brooklyn, patrons can also expect late night jams, water slides, huge swings, nine different camping grounds and even horse vaulting.
