Are these the royal wedding's most glamorous guests?

Princess Eugenie's wedding was a star-studded affair with no shortage of glamorous guests from TV stars to models, but they faced stiff competition from 'polo's dream couple'. 

Bavarian princess Yoanna Otto, 30, and her polo player husband Charlie Hanbury, 32, have been busy documenting their royal wedding celebrations on Instagram in a series of very glamorous snaps. 

German-born Yoanna, the daughter of Princess Anna Oettingen-Wallerstein and her ex-husband Dr York Otto, showcased a variety of enviable designer gowns - all the more impressive as she's nine months pregnant.
