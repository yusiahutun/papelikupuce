Who is Joe Pavelski:

Joe Pavelski is a famous Hockey Player. He was born on July 11, 1984 and his birthplace is Plover, WI. Joe is also well known as, NHL center drafted by the San Jose Sharks in 2006. He was named the Sharks’ captain in 2015 and he led the team to the 2016 Stanley Cup Finals. He won a silver medal with the United States national team at the 2010 Winter Olympics.

Joe is originated from United States. He had three straight multi-goal games in the 2010 Stanley Cup Playoffs, the first player to do so since Mario Lemieux.
