What is the origin of the sigma face meme?

The meme originated from a scene captured in the 2000 film American Psycho. In the movie, characters Patrick Bateman and Paul Allen engage in a conversation while dining. Allen confuses Patrick for someone else and makes a negative comment about him, to which Patrick reacts with an Ooh face.

The sigma face by Patrick Bateman from the movie scene is the meme's origin. People have emulated the facial expression and generated their own meme versions.
