Beyonce and Rihanna Reunite - Beyonc and Rihanna at Diamond Ball

This is what a reunion between two queens looks like:

Getty Images

Last night, Beyoncé and Rihanna came together again at the BadGal's Diamond Ball gala in support of her Clara Lionel Foundation. The black-tie event, which took place in New York City, saw countless celebrities from Leonardo DiCaprio to Cardi B (and even a surprise video call from Barack Obama); but the true stars were these two. Both ladies brought their A-game when dressing for the occasion, with Bey in a sultry emerald wrap dress by House of CB, and Rih in a dramatic high-low gown by Ralph & Russo Couture.

The meeting sent Twitter over the moon:

Rihanna ultimately raised $840,000 last night for her charity, which helps supports children from developing nations around the world to pursue an education. Beyoncé has also been doing philanthropic work with her own organization, BeyGOOD. She recently visited affected areas of Hurricane Harvey in her hometown of Houston, where she and her family met with and served meals to victims of the natural disaster.

Could they be any more perfect?!
