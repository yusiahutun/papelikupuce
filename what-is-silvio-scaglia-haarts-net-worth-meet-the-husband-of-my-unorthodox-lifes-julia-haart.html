What is Silvio Scaglia Haarts net worth? Meet the husband of My Unorthodox Lifes Julia Haart

Netflix has launched a new reality series Real Housewives-inspired, that revolves around the life of the fashion mogul Julia Haart, also featuring her husband and her four children.

Julia Haart is an American fashion designer and entrepreneur, CEO and co-owner of the talent media company “Elite World Group” and the star of Netflix’s latest miniseries.

Having conquered the fashion and business world, Julia Haart opens up about her life on “My Unorthodox Life,” where we get to meet her family, and her husband, Silvio Scaglia Haart.

Silvio Scaglia Haart is a 62-year-old Italian entrepreneur with a net worth of one billion dollars, according to Forbes.

The “Unorthodox Life” of Julia Haart

Premiering on July 14th, 2021 on Netflix, “My Unorthodox Life” is a miniseries that gives an insightful look into the life of Julia Haart, a former member of an orthodox Jewish community, married to a Yeshiva student at the of 19.

The nine-episode series documents Julia and her children’s decision to leave the Haredi Jewish community and pursue their passions for fashion and design.

Haart reports that she had left ultra-Orthodox Judaism over her discomfort with the community’s strict religious observances and principles that she describes as a form of fundamentalism.
