Alec Baldwin and wife make extremely rare public appearance with all seven children

Alec Baldwin and his wife, Hilaria Baldwin stepped out for an extremely rare appearance with all seven of their kids this week.

The Hollywood actor and his entrepreneur wife share seven children together and have been married since 2012. 11 years and seven children later, the couple look as happy as ever.

Alec and Hilaria Baldwin make red carpet appearance with seven kids

Taking to the Annual Hamptons International Film Festival on Thursday, the large family of nine looked happy as they posed for the cameras.

The little ones range from ages 10 to 1 year old. Hilaria, who is 39, gave birth to their youngest, 1-year-old Ilaria last year, as she held her in the snap.

They were also joined by their six other children, Carmen, 10, Rafael, 8, Leonardo,7, Romeo, 5, Eduardo, 3, and María Lucía, 2.

However, it doesn’t look like anymore will be joining the group, as Hilaria revealed in a 2023 interview with Romper, revealed that: Ilaria is “probably, most definitely, almost completely” her last child.

Although the couple has seven children together, Alec actually has eight in total. The movie star is also the father to 27-year-old Ireland Baldwin, who he shares with his ex-wife, Kim Basinger. 

He’s also a grandfather, as Ireland recently gave birth to her first child, Holland, with partner André Allen Anjos.

Alec and Hilaria’s relationship explored

Alec and his first wife Kim, went their separate ways in 2002, and it wasn’t until almost 10 years later that he met his now wife, Hilaria. But how did they meet? In fact, they actually met in a restaurant.

The 97 Minutes star first met his future wife in 2011, and they tied the knot just a year later.

Speaking to the New York Times, Hilaria explained the adorable way the two met, in a New York restaurant, Pure Food and Wine.

Even big celebrities use cheesy pick-up lines apparently as the mom of 7 recalled the moment the two met.

“I was standing near the door with my friends,” Hilaria recalled, “when he walked up and took my hand and said, ‘I must know you,'” she revealed.

However, it was Hilaria who knew Alec, as she was familiar with his work beforehand, as she told PEOPLE she recalls watching him in Working Girl when she was just four years old.
