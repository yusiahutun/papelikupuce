Taylor Swifts Cat Demands Attention in Cute Home Video

Taylor Swift continues to bring the kitty-themed cuteness to her fans with a new video post on her YouTube account. The country singer just got a new cat named Meredith, and she is one adorable little ball of love. In the heartwarming clip, self-proclaimed "cat lady" Swift can be seen getting "trained" by her new pet.

The video shows Swift seated on a comfy chair in a cozy sweater as she pets Meredith. Speaking to the camera, Swift says, "O.K. I'm petting her, petting her, petting her," as she cozies up to her new cat. The cat looks like she's on cloud nine as her owner scratches her, so it's no wonder why she gets a little sad when Swift stops. Every time Swift stops petting her, Meredith lets out a "meow" to let her mama know that she needs more lovin'!

Later on in the short and sweet clip, the 21-year-old superstar says that Meredith is "training" her -- isn't it supposed to be the other way around?! But seriously, how can you not give in to every wish and desire from that precious little fur ball? Heck, we wouldn't be surprised if Swift was still parked in that chair petting Meredith right now!
