How Tall Is Hailey Swirbul Height? American Skier Parents And Family- Does She Have A Boyfriend?

Hailey Swirbul is an American cross-country skier who represents the United States. Scroll down to learn everything about her personal life happenings. 

Hailey Swirbul is one of the most successful American Cross-Country skiers ever at the junior level. Back in 2017, she got the national selection to compete in her first Junior World Championships in Soldier Hollow.

She then got awarded with the first leg in the 4x3,3 km girl’s Relay. After earning her degree, Swirbul appeared in her second World Junior Championships in Goms (SUI).

In addition, she earned silver and bronze medals which made her the most metalized American Cross-Country skier in history at the age of under 20. Hailey has now entered US National team after getting her first World Cup podium in Davos (SUI). 

Swirbul has spent a full-time season at the senior level. Followingly, she also competed in her first World Ski Championships in Oberstdorf last February. While she is preparing for her next championship, let's get to know about her here.

Who Are American Skier Hailey Swirbul Parents? Inside Her Family 

The famous American Skier Hailey Swirbul was born to her parents William Swirbul and Rebecca Edwards Swirbul. She grew up with brother Keegan in a family of four in Carbondale.

Her brother, Keegan is a professional road cyclist who currently rides for UCI ProTeam Rally Cycling. Followingly, he is also a former country skier who competed at the National Championships in Utah in 2013.
