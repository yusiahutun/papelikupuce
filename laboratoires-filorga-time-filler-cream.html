Laboratoires Filorga Time-Filler 5-XP Cream Works Wonders For Wrinkles

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

I’ve written about a lot of skincare products, but it’s so rare to find one that has a 4.9-star overall rating. So when I stumbled upon a formula with a basically perfect score, I knew I had to investigate. The skin savior I’m talking about is the Time-Filler 5-XP Cream.

Laboratoires Filorga recently updated its best-selling Time-Filler formula to work even better on minimizing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. It’s the first time the brand has reformulated the O.G. Time-Filler since it launched a decade ago, so this is a big deal. 

Related Stories

The anti-aging essential now contains five types of expertise: expression lines, deep wrinkles, surface wrinkles, dehydration wrinkles, and neck wrinkles. How can a single face moisturizer address all of that? It’s simple—the Time-Filler 5-XP Cream contains encapsulated hyaluronic acid, tripeptide, sea fennel extract, and kangaroo paw flower extract. That’s an all-star lineup of ingredients if I’ve ever seen one. 

To start, hyaluronic acid is a godsend for retaining moisture since it can hold 1,000 times its weight in water, while tripeptide is packed with amino acids that firm the skin. On top of that, sea fennel extract boosts the production of collagen and kangaroo paw flower extract hydrates, firms, and brightens. All of these ingredients contribute to the product’s anti-aging abilities. The brand calls attention to these four elements, but I took a look at the ingredient list and picked out a few more highlights: glycerin, squalane, and niacinamide. 

Dr. Shereene Idriss, a board-certified dermatologist, the founder of Idriss Dermatology in New York City, and the creator of the #PillowtalkDerm series on Instagram, previously told StyleCaster about the skin benefits of glycerin and explained that “It provides a holy trinity of benefits: it decelerates water loss while offering both skin penetration and retention properties in the dermis.”

As if that wasn’t enough, squalane is great for soothing, softening, and hydrating skin, while niacinamide works to fade dark spots, hydrate, reduce acne and inflammation, and strengthen skin.

After reading about these ingredients’ laundry list of benefits, shoppers’ glowing reviews should come as no surprise. 

“Wow, this is like a little pot of Miracles!!! The luxurious cream has transformed my fine lines and has reduced them dramatically,” raved one happy reviewer. “I have found my holy grail product and won’t ever stop using it. I am a skincare blogger and tried all the top brands like Estée Lauder & Elemis, but this beats them all hands down! I’ve just got the new eye cream to review so can’t wait to try that next.”

Another one wrote, “I’ve only been using this for two weeks now but happy with how my face looks and feels. I’ve already noticed my deep forehead lines diminishing which bothers me the most. Cannot wait to see my results in a month or two and will update with photos.”

“I’m obsessed, my skin feels so hydrated. My wrinkles are reduced tremendously. I’ve bought the entire Time-Filler product line,” another shopper wrote.

If you’re hoping for results like these, apply the Time-Filler 5-XP Cream every day in the morning and night—and remember to show your neck some love, too.

Whether you have deep-set wrinkles or super fine lines coming in around your eyes, this face moisturizer is ready to get to work and yield amazing results. After all, it boasts a 4.9-star overall rating for a reason.
