Not sure what to watch on Monday nights? WWE Universe fans near and far will know that Monday Night Raw is where it’s at. WWE Raw, also known as Monday Night Raw or just Raw, is an American professional wrestling television program that World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) puts on. The flagship show created by Vince McMahon has been airing since January 11, 1993 (over 30 years) and has produced over 1,600 episodes (Season 32 is currently airing). The action-packed showdowns have featured some of WWE’s biggest characters, including but not limited to The Undertaker, “Stone Cold” Steve Austin, Becky Lynch, Triple H, Shawn Michaels, and Charlotte Flair. Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson and John Cena have also appeared on WWE Raw.

The program has broadcasted live from 208 arenas, 171 cities and towns, and ten nations (think the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany, Japan, Italy, and Mexico, among others). Michael Cole provides play-by-play commentary, while Pat McAfee shares color commentary.

Current champions include World Heavyweight Champion Damien Priest, WWE Intercontinental Champion Sami Zayn, Women’s World Champion Rhea Ripley, and Raw Tag Team Champion Awesome Truth (The Miz and R-Truth).
