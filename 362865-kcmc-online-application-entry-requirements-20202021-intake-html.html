KCMC online application and entry requirements for 2020/2021 intake

Kilimanjaro Christian Medical College (KCMC) is among the largest, oldest, and advanced higher learning institution in the Republic of Tanzania. Since its establishment, the university has been providing quality medical training to eligible students irrespective of their backgrounds. That said, the institution is currently open for 2020/2021 KCMC online application in all its courses.

Similar to many institutions of higher education around the world, KCMC has digitized most of its operations. Among them is the online application, which is now accessible to all qualified students. The good news is that you can be among the many students who will be joining the university come 2021. Well, how do you go about the online application process? And what are the requirements for each course?

KCMC University online application

Applicants can apply for the 2020/2021 intake through the KCMC website. Here is the detailed KCMC online application process for new students:

Step 1: Get your registration number

  • Proceed to the online KCMUCo website.
  • On the page, you will be required to sign up.
  • Scroll down the signup box, and click an option labelled "Click here to Get your Registration Number."
  • You will be redirected to a new page.
  • Select the type of your certificate and then proceed to hit the “CLICK HERE TO GET REG NUMBER” button below.

    • Step 2: Fill in the signup details

  • Now use the registration number to create an account on the signup page.
  • Fill all the fields with accurate information and remember to create a strong password. A secure password is one that contains at least one of the following: uppercase, lowercase, numeric digit, and a special symbol.
  • Complete this step by pressing the “REGISTER” button.

    • Step 3: Provide your background data

  • Here, you will provide accurate information as it appears on the original copies. Some of the details to provide include your form IV index number, working mobile number, among many others.
  • Again, press the blue “REGISTER” button.

    Step 4: Sign in

  • Proceed to the login page.
  • Enter your registration number in the first field followed by the password on the second field.
  • Finally, click “LOGIN” to access your account.

    • Step 5: Registration guide and tuition fee payment

    Once you log in, ensure the tuition fee reflects on your account. If not, enter the paying slip number as directed on the university’s homepage. Better yet, you can contact the institute for further assistance.

    The process is slightly different for returning and transferring students. In their case, they can skip step one because they have the registration number already.

    Courses offered at KCMC University and entry requirements

    Similar to most universities, KCMC primarily admits students who are proficient in English. In other words, an applicant should be able to read, write, and speak fluent English. Additional requirements for each course are as follows:

    Diploma courses

    Below are the diploma courses available at KCMC University:

    1. Health Laboratory Science

    Direct entrants should meet the following necessities:

    • Must have three principal passes in Biology, Physics, and Chemistry.
    • Eight (8) minimum entry points in the above subjects is mandatory.
    • Have at least a C in Biology and Chemistry and a D in Physics.

    Equivalently, one must meet the following requirements:

    • Must be a holder of the Certificate of Secondary Education Examination (CSEE) with five passes.
    • Two (2) credit passes in Biology and Chemistry and D in Physics
    • Advanced diploma with an average GPA of 3.5 or B+

    2. Occupational Therapy

    • The four passes should be attained in non-religious subjects.
    • Attain at least a C pass in Biology and Chemistry and a D in Physics.

    Candidates with a pass in mathematics and English language are more likely to be considered for the application.

    Undergraduate

    Here are the undergraduate courses offered in the institution and the entry requirements for each:

    1. Doctor of medicine

    These are the qualifications a direct entrant should meet to be enrolled for this course:

    • Three principal passes in Biology, Physics, and Chemistry
    • A minimum entry of eight (8) points in the above subjects is mandatory.
    • Attain at least a C in Biology and Chemistry and a D in Physics.

    Alternatively one must;

    • Be a CSEE holder with at least five passes including two credit passes in Biology and Chemistry.
    • Have attained grade D in Physics in addition to an appropriate Diploma with an average GPA of 3.5 or B+ OR,
    • Have a BSc in Physics or Zoology or Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology.

    2. BSc in Health Laboratory Science

    To enroll for the course, these are the qualifications direct entrants should meet:

    • Three principal passes in science subjects (Chemistry, Biology, and Physics) with at least 6 points.
    • Attain at least an E in Physics, a D in Biology and C grade in Chemistry.

    Alternatively one should;

    • Be a holder of CSEE with at least 5 passes with two credit passes in Biology and Chemistry.
    • Have a D grade in Physics in addition to an Advanced Diploma with an average GPA of 3.5 or B+, OR
    • BSc majoring in Zoology or Physics or Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology.

    3. BSc of Science in Nursing

    To enroll, one must fulfill the following qualifications:

    • At least six (6) points with three principal passes in Biology, Chemistry and either Mathematics, Physics or Nutrition.
    • A minimum of C grade in Chemistry, a D in Biology, and an E either in Physics, Mathematics, or Nutrition.

    4. BSc in Physiotherapy

    The direct entry qualifications for the course are as follows:

    • A minimum of 6 entry points with three principal passes in Chemistry, Physics, and Biology.
    • At least a C in Chemistry, D in Biology, and an E in Physics.

    Equivalently;

    • One should be a CSEE holder with a minimum of 5 passes.
    • Two credits passes in Biology and Chemistry and a D in Physics in addition to an advanced diploma with an average GPA of 3.5 or B+, OR
    • BSc majoring in Zoology or Physics or Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology
