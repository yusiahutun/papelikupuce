Lindsay Lohan Hits The Red Carpet Bra-less for Gabrielle's Gala!

No bra, no problem!

Lindsay Lohan hit the red carpet for Gabrielle's Gala on Wednesday in London, England. The actress sparkled on the red carpet in her plunging, emerald mini dress, which clearly showed off her decolletage.

The "Mean Girls" star styled her hair straight for the event and sported bronze makeup which really complimented her dress.

Questionable cleavage aside, we're loving Lindsay's sexy look and it's definitely not the first time we've seen the red-headed beauty hit the town without undergarments.

When the 27-year-old stopped by Bravo's "Watch What Happens Live" in April, Andy Cohen asked her asked why she's often shot without a bra on her OWN docuseries "Lindsay."

So what's the reason? Plain and simple, Lindsay doesn't "like them." Well, more power to her -- if the actress' comfortable in her own skin, who are we to judge?
