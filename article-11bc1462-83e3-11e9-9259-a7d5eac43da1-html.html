Laurel teen killed walking Okla. highway | News

A 17-year-old Laurel boy was struck and killed while walking on an Oklahoma highway early Thursday morning, according to Oklahoma Highway Patrol officials.

Dawson Williams was struck by a Chevrolet Tahoe driven by Cristy Rose, 42, of Chickasha, Okla. She and her 21-year-old passenger, April N. Nelson of Chickasha, were not injured, according to the report by OHA public information officers.
