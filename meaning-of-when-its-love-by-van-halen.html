Meaning of When Its Love by Van Halen

The Van Halen song, 'When It's Love,' beautifully encapsulates the complex and mysterious nature of love. Love is a universal concept that everyone seeks, yet struggles to define. This rock anthem explores the enigmatic aspects of love and the introspective journey one embarks upon to understand its true essence.Throughout the song, Van Halen raises profound questions about the nature of love and offers glimpses into the answers. One of the standout lyrics in 'When It's Love' is, "How do I know when it's love? I can't tell you but it lasts forever." This line introduces the overarching theme of the song: the enduring and timeless nature of true love. It suggests that love is not a fleeting emotion but a profound state of being that transcends time and space.In the first verse, the lyrics, "Everybody's looking for something, something to fill in the holes," convey a sense of longing and incompleteness that drives individuals to seek love. It touches upon the idea that love is not merely a romantic pursuit but also a profound human need. The song suggests that love has the power to heal the voids within our souls and make us whole.The chorus lyrics, "How does it feel when it's love? It's just something you feel together," delve into the theme of unity and connection. Love is not a solitary experience but something that is shared between two people. It emphasizes the idea that love is an intangible force that strengthens the bond between individuals, creating a unique and powerful connection.Another interesting lyric comes in the second verse, "Waiting for someone to come into focus, teach you your final love lesson." This verse explores the transformative aspect of love. It suggests that love is not only a source of joy and fulfillment but also an instrumental force that shapes our lives and teaches us invaluable lessons. Love has the power to change people, challenging them to grow and discover new aspects of themselves.The refrain, "When it's love, you can feel it, yeah, nothing's missing, yeah," introduces the theme of completeness. Love is depicted as a state in which nothing is lacking, where all needs are fulfilled. It suggests that when love is present, there is a sense of wholeness and contentment that permeates every aspect of life.The guitar solo within the song adds a unique dimension to the overall themes. It serves as a creative representation of the emotional intensity and passionate energy that love can bring. The solo allows the listener to experience the exhilaration and raw power that love can elicit, further enhancing the song's exploration of the complex nature of this profound emotion.In summary, 'When It's Love' by Van Halen delves into the enigmatic nature of love, its enduring qualities, and the transformation it brings to individuals. Through its poignant lyrics, the song highlights the themes of longing, unity, transformation, completeness, and intensified emotions. It encourages listeners to ponder the profound mysteries of love and prompts them to embark on their own introspective journey towards understanding this intricate emotion.
