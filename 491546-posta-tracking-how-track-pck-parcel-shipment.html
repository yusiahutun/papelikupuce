Posta tracking: How to track your PCK parcel and shipment

The Postal Corporation of Kenya, Posta, has been a reliable logistics corporation in Kenya for many years. The company offers shipment services, picking up your items from a specified location and dropping them at your desired destination. It can send parcels through its local post offices or internationally. Depending on the item you want to send, Posta can deliver it within the same day or up to three months for surface parcels. However, as you wait for your items, you can use the Posta tracking portal to track your shipment. But how do you do it?

When the Postal Corporation of Kenya accepts to transit your item from the point of pickup, it enters the item’s data into the Posta parcel tracking portal. Then, you will receive a shipment ID once you have made the required shipping payment.

You will need the shipment ID to track your shipment from the pickup station to the destination. The tracking portal will have all the information you need regarding your item and when to expect its arrival.

How to track your parcel and shipment using Posta tracking

The Postal Corporation of Kenya makes it easy for customers to track a parcel in Kenya from the pickup point to the destination. You can track a package or shipment using two methods. Here is a look at each method.

Track Posta parcels and shipments online

The Posta tracking service is available online. It offers the fastest way of determining the location of your shipment. Furthermore, it gives you complete tracking information, including the office from which the parcel originated. You will also view the location of your shipment and the destination. To track your shipment:

  • Ensure you have a shipment ID. Your shipment ID is on the receipt you received when booking for shipment.
  • Go to the Posta tracking website.
  • Enter your shipment ID in the space provided.
  • Your shipment ID is 13 characters long.
  • Press enter to get your shipment’s tracking information.

The information displayed will include the current location of your parcel. In addition, PCK keeps the tracking information for the next 120 days, even after the corporation has delivered your shipment. That will make it easier for you to refer to the information in case of disputes.

Track Posta parcel through email

You can also track your parcel or shipment via email. That means you will have to contact Posta by sending an email. Here is what to do:

  • Send an email to customerservice@posta.co.ke.
  • Provide your shipment ID.
  • Wait for Posta to reply.

PCK will reply with all the information you need about your shipment.

Once your Posta delivers your item, it will send you an SMS or Email notification. The SMS notification only applies to customers in Kenya.

Does Posta Kenya offer history tracking?

Yes, the Postal Corporation of Kenya allows you to check the history of your past package transactions. It is a valuable feature that makes it easier to get information on the packages you have transported in the past using the carrier. However, the feature is only available to registered customers who use the Posta Lastmile service.

The Posta Lastmile service is a door-to-door delivery service only available in Nairobi County, Kenya. It only delivers three types of items, including passports, personal items, parcels, EMS, or letters. Kenya Posta tracking is available for customers that use the service.

How do I contact Posta Kenya?

If you want to get more information about post office parcel tracking, you can contact Posta Kenya as follows:

  • Pilot lines: 0719072000, 07306420000, or 0203242000
  • Customer care: 0719072600, 0730642600, or 0734108120
  • Email: info@posta.co.ke

Alternatively, you can visit the Postal Corporation of Kenya headquarters at the Post Office GPO Branch, PR79+HF5, Kenyatta Ave, Nairobi, Kenya.
