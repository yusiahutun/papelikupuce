10 powerful prayers for new month blessings and a fresh start

Every new month comes with new blessings. The beginning of a new month means that God has something new for His people. Every Christian should always begin the month with prayers. The prayers are a way of speaking into our new month as we enter into it. When you make prayers for new month blessings, you allow God to guide your month and submit to His will.
