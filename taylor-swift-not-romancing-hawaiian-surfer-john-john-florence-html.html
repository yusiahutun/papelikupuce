'They're not dating... or texting': Taylor Swift is NOT romancing Hawaiian surfer John John Florence

She's certainly seems to have a thing for curly-haired, baby-faced boys.

But despite reports that she has started dating Hawaiian surfer John John Florence, sources close to Taylor Swift have denied the claims.

It was reported on Wednesday that the 23-year-old singer was attempting to give love another chance, and had been text messaging the hunky blonde surfer.

No truth: Taylor Swift is not dating Hawaiian surfer John John Florence, despite reports suggesting otherwise

However, a source told E! News of the Life & Style Magazine report: 'There is no truth to this at all. Taylor is not dating - or texting - this guy.'

Rumours of a romance between the pair were fuelled when 20-year-old John John was asked about the relationship on Australian television.

'Uh, I’m not supposed to comment about that,' he said.

Skilled: The sportman's skills on the surfboard have been described as 'unprecedented'

Smiling, he added: 'Yeah, you know, it's kind of a secret thing going on right now. Can't bust it out in the public just yet.'

Taylor is famous for penning songs about her ex-boyfriends.

She finally revealed this week that her hit single I Knew You Were Trouble is about her ex-boyfriend Harry Styles.

Beware a woman scorned: Taylor admitted recently that her hit single I Knew You Were Trouble was about her ex-boyfriend Harry Styles

She has a type: Taylor, pictured with her ex-boyfriend Conor Kennedy, appears to have a penchant for curly-haired blondes

Speaking about her spirited performance at the 2012 Brit Awards in front of Harry and his One Direction bandmates, the singer was asked how she felt performing the number while her ex was watching.

Taylor told The Sunday Times: 'Well, it’s not hard to access that emotion when the person the song is directed at is standing by the side of the stage watching.'
