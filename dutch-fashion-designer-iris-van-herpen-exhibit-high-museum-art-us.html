Dutch fashion designer Iris van Herpen to exhibit at High Museum of Art in the US

Dutch fashion designer Iris van Herpen's work will soon find a home in the High Museum of Art in the United States. The designer, who has dressed the likes of Lady Gaga, Beyoncé and Björk, will feature 45 outfits carefully selected from her collections between 2008 and 2015. Featured will be one-of-a-kind haute couture pieces and some of the world's first examples of 3D printed fashion.

This marks the first time that an American museum has exhibited the designer's work on such a large scale. It is co-organised with the Netherlands' Groninger Museum and will continue on a North American tour.
