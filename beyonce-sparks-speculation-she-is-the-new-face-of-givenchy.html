Is Beyonc the new face of Givenchy?

    Beyoncé has sparked speculation that she is the new face of Givenchy after the 33-year-old was spotted on a photoshoot with the brand's creative director Riccardo Tisci. Clad head-to-toe in Givenchy, the pop star posed for the camera on a rooftop in Brooklyn, occasionally taking a break to chat with Riccardo.

    Beyoncé and Jay Z at the 2015 Met Ball wearing Givenchy

    Only a few days before, Beyoncé was championing the French fashion house at the 2015 Met Ball. Posing with rapper husband Jay Z, Beyoncé topped best-dressed lists around the world as she showed off her incredible figure in a dazzling sheer gown with bejewelled embellishment.

    This marks the fourth year in a row that the singer has chosen to wear Givenchy for the glitzy event. Although there has been no confirmation that she will be the brand's new ambassador, there is no doubt that Beyoncé already shares a close relationship with Givenchy, and Riccardo.

    It wasn't just her gown that had heads turning at the Met Ball – jaws dropped at Beyoncé's enviably toned figure and world-famous curves. The singer has since revealed the secret behind her slender body, admitting she has been sticking to vegan diet the 22 Day Revolution diet, created by Marco Borges.

    Riccardo Tisci escorted Julianne Moore to the 2015 Met Ball

    "The truth is that if a Houston-born foodie like me can do it, you can too," the singer wrote in the foreword of the nutritionist's new book which explains the diet. "You just need to try it for 22 days."

    The diet, favoured by other stars including Jennifer Lopez, is plant-based with a mixture of whole foods, and bans meat, eggs, dairy, alcohol or processed foods. Beyoncé first started the regime in November 2013, with rapper husband Jay Z also taking part. Since then, the singer claims to have lost 65lbs, noting that she is in the 'best shape' of her life.

    "All the meals are incredibly unique and delicious," Marco told HELLO! "It would be difficult to pick just one but some of our [Beyoncé and Jay Z] favorites include the hearty breakfast granola, home-style split-pea and vegetable soup, Spanish style paella and gluten-free pesto pasta."
