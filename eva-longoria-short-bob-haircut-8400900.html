Eva Longoria Just Cut Her Hair So Short

In case you had any doubts, bobs are still the haircut du jour — from Selena Gomez to Zendaya, celebrities can’t stop cutting off all of their hair. And, now, Eva Longoria is the latest celebrity to go for the big chop.

Last week, Longoria attended the Academy Women's Luncheon presented by Chanel in Los Angeles, debuting her drastically fresh new cut. Proving the power of a major hair transformation, she was almost unrecognizable, as she styled her shoulder-length layered lob straight with a middle part. The actress paired her sleek bob with a head-to-toe Chanel look, which consisted of a black sequined long-sleeved shirt tucked into a coordinating pair of pleated puddle pants. A black-and-white tweed jacket in a houndstooth pattern was layered over her shoulders, while her accessories included a black quilted handbag and a smattering of silver necklaces by the brand.

Later that evening, Eva arrived at the 2023 Women of the Year awards for Glamour Magazine and styled her new cut the same way — but this time, pairing it with a sparkly silver sequined gown. Keeping her hair the main focus, she added a just pair of diamond stud earrings and a bold red lip to her second look.

A few days after debuting her shoulder-grazing lob, Eva reminded fans of her signature long hair with a throwback photo shared to Instagram. "11:11 manifest the moment," she wrote alongside a snap of herself with bust-length loose waves while wearing a bra top beneath a coordinating sparkly blush-toned blazer and matching trousers.
