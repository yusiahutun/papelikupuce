Hunger Games Nail Polish Colors from CoverGirl

Catching Fire, the latest addition to The Hunger Games trilogy, is in theaters Friday and Katniss fever is in full swing. And because the YA lit has racked up diehard fans spanning cross all age groups, getting in on the hype has universal appeal—exactly why we couldn't wait to get our hands on the super vibrant Cover Girl Capitol Collection Glosstinis. Think vibrant and multi-faceted green, fuschia, purple, auburn, orange and red, each perfect for any woman who wants a mani that's, well, on fire. And the best part: the teeny bottles let you test-drive one (or all) of the shades and even get the chance to finish every last drop before the final series in the threequel debuts.

Cover Girl Outlast Stay Brilliant Glosstinis (seen here in Flamed Out, Seared Bronze, Scalding Emerald, Rogue Red, Volet Flicker, Pyro Pink, Inferno, Sulfur Blaze, Black Heat), $3.79, at drugstores.
