The climate in the United States differs significantly based on various factors such as latitude and diverse geographical features such as mountains and deserts. Texas is the second-largest state after Alaska in land area, with more than 1,200 incorporated cities. Aside from that, the famous state has a reputation for being hot, dry, and too sunny most of the time.
