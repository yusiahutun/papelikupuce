Lola Consuelos Jokes Parents Kelly Ripa and Mark Are Absolutely Repulsive After Flirty Comment

Celebrity couple Kelly Ripa and Mark Consuelos‘ PDA still grosses out their 18-year-old daughter, Lola Consuelos. She most recently spoke out about her parents’ flirty exchanges over Instagram when Kelly, 49, shared a video of herself lifting up her 2020 Oscars gown to reveal producer Albert Bianchini hiding underneath.

“Been there …,” Mark, 48, hilariously wrote in the comments. However, his daughter didn’t think it was so funny.

“Absolutely repulsive,” Lola wrote back, clearly feeling perturbed.

Kelly captioned the video “Oil change” and, for the 92nd Academy Awards, she wore a custom made ball gown by none other than Project Runway‘s Christian Siriano. Although Lola didn’t attend the Hollywood event with her parents, Kelly made sure to document their night out on Instagram.

She shared one photo of her and Mark on the red carpet and another of her beau at the Vanity Fair after party. Lola didn’t appear to have any problem with those snaps, but she did mind when her mom “shut down” her debit card for ordering too much Postmates.

“I didn’t know our daughter had Postmates,” Kelly revealed on her talk show. “She’s at college and we signed her up for a meal plan, cause we’re not monsters. We signed her up for a meal program.”

“But you know, she doesn’t like the meals at school so she was ordering Postmates,” the TV personality added. “Here’s what would happen. She would order the $7 salad but it would cost $25 dollars to have it delivered three blocks in New York City.”

Due to the high delivery rates, Kelly made the executive decision to halt her daughter’s spending habits. “Oh, we shut down that debit card account she had,” Kelly said.

We would have definitely done the same thing, but we wonder if Kelly and Mark will dial down their PDA a bit — at least for Lola’s sake?
