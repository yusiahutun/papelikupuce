Who Is Andreea Dragoi Mom Lucy Dragoi? Her Age, Job

San Jose State swimmer and model Andreea Dragoi recently went viral on Instagram and TikTok racking up thousands of followers in the last month only. Now, her mother Lucy Dragoi, who is a pageant star,  has also started receiving attention from the netizens.

Continue reading to know more about Andreea’s mom, Lucy Dragoi, including information about her age, job, husband, relationship status, etcetera.

Meet Lucy Dragoi, Andreea Dragoi Mom

Andreea Dragoi began making waves last Christmas as she emerged as a college influencer for San Jose State University last year. While her presence was quiet then, Andreea started having comparisons with TikTok star and influencer Olivia Dunne. Dubbed ‘Olivia Dunne of the Pool,’ Andreea captured the attention of people on the Internet alike.

In the 2022-23 season, she finished 31st overall with a final time of 1:43.50 in the 200-medley relay at the CSCAA National Invitational Championship; took second position in the 200-yard butterfly with a 2:08.11 against UC Davis; clocked a 59.37 in the 100-yard butterfly to take first in the event against CSU East Bay; and recorded a time of 2:05:50 to finish in the top 10 of the 200-yard Butterfly at the Mizzou Invite

She hails from Bucharest, Romania, and got her IB diploma from the International School of Bucharest. In her hometown, she competed in the World and European Junior Championships. Andreea decided to attend SJSU because of its kinesiology, sport management, and psychology programs.

As a junior at San Jose State, Andreea decided to spend her summer as a lifeguard in Florida. To announce her new stint, Andreea posted a video on TikTok, which sort of went viral.

20-year-old Andreea is a model too; she modeled at Bucharest Fashion Week in 2021. She won the Miss Ambassador US Nation Beauty Pageant and Miss California U.S. Nation Pageant. In April 2023, Andreea was announced as the new host of The Ball Out

Instagram sensation Andreea took a break from her job as a lifeguard and attended a party in Miami. While she turned many heads, her mother Lucy Dragoi who was there with her daughter also received a lot of attention.

Andrea donned a white dress to the party whereas Lucy was seen in a black top and a jeans miniskirt. Numerous people commented on the picture that she posted to Instagram with one user even flirting with her asking “I’m not sure the question was asked.is there a Mr. Dragoi,? Asking for a friend.”
