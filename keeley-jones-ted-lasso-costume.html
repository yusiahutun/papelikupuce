6 Keeley Jones Costumes to Show Your Love for 'Ted Lasso'

If you're searching for easy Halloween costume inspiration, look no further than Keely Jones, entrepreneur, fashion maven, and star of the AppleTV+ series Ted Lasso. Keeley (portrayed by scene-stealer Juno Temple) is hands-down the most fashionable character on the show, and any of her looks could easily provide costume inspiration. Her quirky sense of style is seen in everything from her business attire to her pajamas. If you're looking for a group costume, check out our roundup of Ted Lasso costumes for looks from all of the characters.

Looking for a or couple's costume? Pair one of these Keeley ideas with a Roy Kent or a Jamie Tartt (but which one will you choose?). If you're feeling bold, go for Keeley dressed as a lion from season 1!
