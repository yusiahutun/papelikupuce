How to download KCSE result slip and print it

Don’t know how to download your KCSE result slip? You don’t need to worry about this. In this article, it is clearly mentioned how to download your KCSE result slip online and offline. Through this article, you can get easy options to view/ download/ print your KCSE result slip.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

  • How to download KCSE result slip

What is KCSE?

KCSE stands for Kenya Certificate Secondary Education. The KCSE exam is taken after completion of secondary education. The examination usually takes place in between the month of October and November and the results are released in the month of February by the Ministry of Education. KCSE results are announced to the public.

How to download KCSE result slip

  • To download KCSE result slip you need to have a stable internet connection. Computer is the easy option to access the internet. You can also access the internet via mobile phone but some of the websites do not work properly on the mobile phone. So, the computer is a good option to access the internet for checking your result.
  • KCSE Index number- To know the result you need your KCSE Index number. Without your KCSE Index number, you cannot view or download your result.

The students can download their KCSE result slip online through the website of KNEC and they can also download their KCSE result slip through SMS by sending their Index number to 22252.

KNEC KCSE result slip download options

Want to know how to download your KCSE online result slip? - Mobile technology has made simpler for students. Isn’t it? Just a few clicks away! Now through SMS, you can easily access the KCSE result slip download option.

1. KCSE result slip – Online

To download and print your KCSE result slip online, you have to follow few steps. The steps are given below:

  • Open the KNEC portal website.
  • At the website, there are many options given, you have to choose the option “examination results”.
  • At the first drop-down list, you have to choose KCSE examination and at the second drop-down list, you have to choose the year.
  • Then a text box will appear, you need to give your KCSE Index number and then click on ‘submit’ button.
  • After this, the result is displayed on your screen.
