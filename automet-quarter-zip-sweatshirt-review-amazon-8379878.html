I Dont Want to Take Off This Soft and Cozy $24 Amazon Sweatshirt

It’s that point in the season where it’s too cold to go without a jacket but too warm to wear a coat — which means it’s sweatshirt season. I remember having longer transitional periods between fall and winter in previous years, but these days, you’re lucky if you get a few weeks before it’s time to break out your puffer coat. So recently, I’ve been reaching for my Automet Quarter-Zip Sweatshirt, which keeps me comfortable at just the right temperature and is one of the softest transitional pieces I own. Now, just $24, I plan on picking up a few more shades while I can still wear them without needing a coat.

The quarter-zip sweatshirt comes in 17 colors, including gray, blue, pink, purple, and white in sizes S through XL. It features a breathable and soft cotton-blend fabric with a fleece lining, spacious front kangaroo pouch front-pocket, and a deep hoodie for extra head coverage. The fit is oversized, sitting away from the body without looking bulky, and the arms have a classic raglan sleeve with a thumb cutout at the cuffs. 

Automet Quarter-Zip Sweatshirt 

My favorite thing about this pullover sweatshirt is how plush the fabric is, so much that it feels like I’m wrapped up in a blanket. The zipper is long enough that I don’t have to struggle to pull it up over my head or worry about it snagging on my hair. I also love that the sleeves have finger holes because I find that I naturally curl them into the cuffs of my sweatshirts. It’s perfectly oversized so that you can layer tanks and tees underneath without looking messy, and the hood is deep enough to place over my head while wearing a ponytail. 

Styling this piece is also a no-brainer; I pair it with mom jeans, a white T-shirt, and sneakers or loafers. But I also love a good all-gray outfit, so I often match my gray sweatshirt with sweatpants in the same hue. And on days when the weather fluctuates, it also looks chic draped over my shoulders.
