The Liberty beauty Christmas crackers are back

The Liberty Beauty Crackers have returned for Christmas 2019, with three to choose from this year.

The ultimate luxury table decorations, stocking fillers or 'secret Santa' gifts, each cracker comes dressed in versions of the new Liberty Christmas print with matching paper crowns and gift tags.

Packing gems from the iconic Liberty London Beauty Hall, they're themed by self-care, granting a head-to-toe glow and party beauty glam.

Aromatherapy Associates and Susanne Kaufmann, £14 (worth £22)

Courtesy

This beauty cracker contains a full size Aromatherapy Associates De-Stress Rollerball (10ml) and the Susanne Kaufmann Calming St. John’s Wort Bath (50g).

OUAI and Frank Body, £14 (worth £18.95)

Courtesy

This houses the OUAI Rose Hair & Body Oil (45ml), and a full size Frank Body Shimmer Lip Gloss (15ml).

Kester Black and Bobbi Brown, £14 (worth £27.50)

Courtesy

Inside this cracker you'll find the Bobbi Brown Smokey Eye Mascara in Black (3ml), plus a full size Kester Black Nail Polish in Dasher (15ml).

Covetable inside and out, as with the (now sold out) Liberty London Beauty Advent Calendar, you'll be hard pressed not to fall for these charming crackers.
