Kris Jenner, 67, cuts a stylish figure in black tweed dress and knee-high boots as she leaves Costes

Kris Jenner wowed in an all black ensemble as she left Costes restaurant in Paris on Friday night. 

The reality star, 67, looked stylish in a black button-up tweed dress, which she styled with black knee-high boots.

She toted her essentials in a small black handbag as she appeared to be flanked by security while stepping out of the celebrity hotspot.

Kris showed off her ageless beauty as she donned a heavy eye make up look for the glitzy night in Paris alongside her partner Corey Gamble.

The Kardashian matriarch was among the A-list stars to attend Victoria Beckham's show for Paris Fashion Week earlier in the day.
