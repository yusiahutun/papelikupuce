'I was on Naked Attraction and invited pals over to watch me strip on cheeky show'

A brave Naked Attraction contestant invited her friends to watch her strip-off on the show.

The concept of whipping off your clothes in the hopes of securing a date while the rest of the country watches from their living room is a daunting concept for many. But one ballsy dater was prepared to let her nearest and dearest see them in the nude by hosting a watch party.

The racy Channel 4 dating show leaves nothing to the imagination and many would cringe at the idea of revealing their naked body in front of thousands, maybe millions, of viewers. But for an anonymous player the programme served as a boost in self-esteem.
