Elvis Presley's Granddaughter Riley Keough Marries Ben-Smith Petersen - Closer Weekly

Congratulations, Riley Keough!

Elvis Presley‘s granddaughter, 25, has reportedly married stuntman Ben Smith-Peterson in Napa, Calif., on Wednesday, Feb. 4 after dating for about a year, according to ‘Us Weekly.’

Ben announced his engagement to the actress last August when he posted a photo of Riley — whose mom is Elvis and Priscilla Presley’s daughter, Lisa Marie Presley — showing off her ring on Instagram.

The couple reportedly met in 2012 while filming the action film ‘Mad Max: Fury Road.’

Riley and Ben at a gala in May 2014. (Photo Credit: Getty)

Riley was previously engaged to her ‘Magic Mike’ costar Alex Pettyfer in 2012 but they split later that year.

The star is Lisa Marie’s eldest child — she also has a son, Benjamin, 22 — with ex-husband Danny Keough, whom she was married to from 1988 – 1994. Riley additionally has two half-sisters, twins Harper and Finley, 7, from her mother’s marriage to Michael Lockwood.

‘Us Weekly‘ confirmed the news of Riley and Ben’s marriage.
