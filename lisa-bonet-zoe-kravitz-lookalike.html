Lisa Bonet and her daughter Zoe Kravitz could pass as twins

No, you're not seeing double – Lisa Bonet and Zoe Kravitz really could pass as twins. The pair may be eligible for the most convincing mother-daughter lookalikes in Hollywood, beating Katie Holmes and Suri Cruise, Reese Witherspoon and her mini-me Ava, Gwyneth Paltrow and her daughter Apple, and Goldie Hawn and Kate Hudson.

On Wednesday, Lisa and Zoe were pictured attending the star-studded 2016 Saint Laurent at the Palladium Event in Los Angeles, with Zoe's dad Lenny Kravitz. And it was the striking similarity between mother and daughter that had passers-by talking.

Zoe Kravitz, 27, and Lisa Bonet, 48, could pass as twins

Fans wouldn't have been able to guess that Lisa, who was married to her ex-husband Lenny for six years, has a 21-year age difference with her daughter.

Her "twin" and mini-me Zoe shares the same gorgeous high cheekbones, dark brown almond-shaped eyes and slender frame. The pair even decided to wear their hair in similar styles for the event.

Lisa and Lenny Kravitz were married for six years

Exuding confidence in front of the cameras, both Lisa and Zoe looked effortlessly cool and relaxed. While the former star of The Cosby Show opted for a shimmery, sequinned floor-length dress, her 27-year-old daughter Zoe rocked a yellow dress and leather jacket.

Lenny was on hand to walk his girls down the red carpet, wearing his trademark leather trousers, shades and a colourful T-shirt.

Other big names from the music world in attendance included Sam Smith, Justin Bieber and Lady Gaga, while Jessica Alba, Sylvester Stallone and Demi Moore represented Hollywood.
