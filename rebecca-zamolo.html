Rebecca Zamolo Bio, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Rebecca Zamolo is an American YouTuber and social media personality. She started of her career working in commercials and now is a social media sensation. She has been married to Matt Slays since 2014. Rebecca is managed by Eric Black at Crestview Entertainment.

Early Life

Rebecca Lynn Zamolo was born on September 28, 1982, in Martinez, California, the United States to parents Paul and Cathy Zamolo. She has five siblings namely, Jennifer, Monica, Michael, and Lissa, Rebecca is the oldest of them all. Rebecca graduated from the University of California with a degree in communications and sports management. She even severed as the sports broadcaster for her university station Gaucho News. Zamolo suffered from inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) for nearly eight years, she has even made a documentary on this journey.

Rebecca Zamolo Career

From a young age of nice, Zamolo started her training as a gymnast. Her sheer dedication and hard work made her winning regional and national championships. Having been a gymnast, it was very easy for her to start cheerleading, thus making her part of the San Jose Sabercats where she was one of the professional cheerleaders. During her time as cheerleader, she also started to give auditions which landed her to appear in various commercials. Later she also became a sports broadcaster for the college station Gaucho News.

After graduation, Rebecca moved to Los Angeles to pursue her career. She worked at The Second City and then in 2010 was seen regularly on The Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson. Later, Rebecca switched to social media and started to make her own videos and content. She joined the video platform, Tik-Tok and within no time the audience loved her work. Rebecca gained immense popularity on the app with 1600 million likes and 283 fans as of 2020. She is usually seen showcasing her gymnast’s skills, freestyle dance, funny content, lip-syncs, and collaborations.

In 2014 she started her YouTube channel which as of 2020 has 9.58 million subscribers with total of 2 billion views. She is a part of The Real Game Master which is a network of friends doing challenges and tasks, both Matt and Rebecca take part in them and even upload them on their channels. Her most video if from season one of The Real Game Master named Escaping Abandoned Prison with more than 34 million views. She has worked with Matt Steffanina, Cloe Couture, Sharlene Taule, Matthew Morrison, Dean Norris, and many other artists.
