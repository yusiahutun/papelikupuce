Kendall Jenner just wore this Fashion Editor approved designer It bag

*Adds to cart

    Kendall Jenner has become as renowned for her personal style as she has for her modelling prowess, and so it’s no surprise that her latest accessory is about to become the most coveted it bag of 2022.

    In a series of photos shared with her 263 million followers, the 26-year-old supermodel donned Prada’s Symbole Triangle bag, with the caption "city strolls." Ever the bookworm, Kendall showed off the bag on her daily travels to the library and out and about, ending with running errands at the florist. We love a versatile queen.

    The bag is set to be the next influencer favourite accessory

    Prada’s aptly named Triangolo Mini jacquard shoulder bag is defined by its triangular jacquard patterning and shape. Trimmed with leather, it has a chain shoulder strap and a zipped front. The silhouette of the bag celebrates the Italian luxury house’s origins, proudly boasting the shape of it’s in the shape of the iconic triangle-shaped logo. "A true emblem of Prada’s identity with its essential silhouette and universal shape, the triangle logo inspires the modernist pattern decorating the Prada Symbole bag," the brand explains on its website. 

    View post on Instagram 

    This isn't the first time the brand has captivated the fashion set, their Cleo leather bag and raffia tote bag has dominated the feed of every influencer's Instagram account for the last two years. Expect the triangle to become just as coveted and desired as its predecessors, especially after being championed by the model turned tequila entrepreneur.
