About

Natalia Osipova is a 37 year old Russian Ballet dancer. Born Natalia Petrovna Osipova on 18th May, 1986 in Moscow, Russia and educated at Moscow State Academy of Choreography (The Bolshoi Ballet Academy), she is famous for being the principal dancer of the Royal Ballet London, and for her performances in Giselle, Don Quixote, The Nutcracker, Swan Lake, The Sleeping Beauty, Onegin, Mayerling, Anastasia, La Bayadère, La Sylphide, Esmerelda, and Solo for Two. Her zodiac sign is Taurus.

Natalia Osipova is a member of the following lists: Russian ballet dancers, 1986 births and Prix Benois de la Danse winners.
