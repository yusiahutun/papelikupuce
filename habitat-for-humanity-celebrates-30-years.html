Habitat for Humanity Celebrates 30 Years

Catherine Spiller, Madeline Greenwood, Marc Bozone and Marie Dick

More than 200 people attended Habitat for Humanity of Martin County’s 30Years of ‘Building on Faith’ celebration, held in the event room at Habitat for Humanity of Martin County’s new ReStore in Jensen Beach. Guests were treated to heavy hors d’ourves, cocktails, along with a video documenting the organization’s work throughout the community during the past three decades.
