Sundays' No.15 10-Free Polish Review

If someone had told me 10 years ago that red would become my go-to nail color, I'd tell them they're lying. But here we are.

And while I haven't completely abandoned my beloved nude polishes, sundays' No.15 has got me in a chokehold — and I don't want to be freed.

To me, this color is the perfect shade of red. It's bright, but not too bright. It pops, but looks gorgeous against all skin tones. And the lacquer itself is creamy, smooth, and incredibly easy to not screw up.

When it comes down to the formula, it's not only made from a blend of vegan ingredients, but it's also 10-free, meaning it has no traces of controversial ingredients like TPHP, dibutyl phthalate, toluene, xylene, ethyl tosylamide, camphor, formaldehyde, or formaldehyde resin. However, that doesn't mean that the polish itself lacks when it comes to results.

To shop: $18; dearsundays.com

Since I typically go with gel polish for my fingernails, No.15 has become a staple for my toes, and when I tell you it does not chip. Girl ...

I have a bad habit of tripping over uneven pavement while in sandals, banging my toes against tables, and showering several times a day (yes, that's shade), so my feet are constantly in water. Yet, the polish doesn't budge until I'm ready to take it off myself.
