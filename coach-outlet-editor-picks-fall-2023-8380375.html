I Buy Designer Bags for Up to 67% Off From This Little-Known Outlet

It wasn’t until recently that I started collecting bags. For years I carried the same leather crossbody and before that I opted for whatever was cute (and cheap) at Target. But even when I wasn’t actively buying new purses, I was coveting them, with Coach’s iconic bags living rent free in my mind (though not yet in my closet, unfortunately). But this year I discovered the brand’s little-known online outlet store and I am now prepared to cross quite a few items off my wishlist for less.

Coach Outlet gives you access to a number of the brand’s most famous bags, including canvas totes, leather shoulder bags, backpacks, and crossbody purses for a fraction of the price. City Tote in Signature Canvas

If you’re wanting something distinctly Coach, I’m personally obsessed with the City Tote in the brand’s Signature Canvas, which boasts more than 1,300 five-star ratings. This bag features the iconic “C” print, is available in nine color combinations and, according to shoppers, is “very spacious.” “It’s my to go for work and everyday,” said one shopper customer who wrote that, thanks to the size, it can “double as a work bag and [their] mommy bag.” “It's super functional,” wrote another fan, who explained that it fits their “laptop, big wallet, and books,” making it perfect for work, school, and travel.

Ellie File Bag

If you prefer something smaller (and less logo-heavy), consider the Ellie File Bag. This crossbody purse is crafted out of pebble leather and available in three classic colors: black, white, and brown. “I use it any time,” wrote one shopper about the “versatile” bag, writing that they’ve taken it to “formal” dinners and on errands. And another person loved how the sleek but simple bag is “easy to combine with [their] clothing.” As for the size, customers say that it fits all their “daily essentials perfectly plus some.” “[There’s] ample room for a large wallet, cosmetic bag, [and] eyeglasses.”

Teri Shoulder Bag Signature Quilting

Silver has quickly become 2023’s hottest color. And while many have opted to wear the metallic shade on their feet, including Katie Holmes and Martha Stewart, I’m thinking of wearing it on my shoulder with the quilted Teri Bag. This trendy piece, which comes with both a shoulder and crossbody strap, features chain detailing and a giant “C” at the bag’s center. It’s a look-at-me purse perfect for a night out (imagine this, a LBD, and silver pumps). One fan deemed this the “perfect party bag,” writing that it’s “ritzy looking” for any event, “whether business or casual,” and fits all the essentials.

Orchid Boot

Since seeing Naomi Watts in a pair of chic black boots last week, I’ve been really wanting a pair of my own. And right now, Coach’s Orchid Boots are at the top of my list. These leather knee-highs offer a clean, classic silhouette with standout details — including a not-too-slim, not-too-chunky 3-inch heel and a small belt around the ankle — and are currently 46 percent off.
