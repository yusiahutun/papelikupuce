    How to Choose the Best e.l.f. Setting Spray?

    Here are the parameters to consider before getting your hands on the best e.l.f. setting spray —

    a) Formula- If you have sensitive skin, a soothing formula that will minimize irritation and redness would work great for you. Similarly, for dry skin types, a hydrating formula would work the best. Lastly, for people with oily skin, a non-comedogenic formula would work wonders.

    b) Finish- The finish you want out of your makeup look depends a lot on your preference. However, it is best to go for a matte finish if you have oily skin. On the other hand, pick a dewy formula, if you have dry to very dry skin for a natural finish.

    c) Ingredients- Ingredients play a great role in picking out your best product. A setting spray packed with potent ingredients like aloe vera and green tea and cucumber extracts will set your makeup while giving your skin the much-needed hydration and nourishment that it deserves. 

    Now let's check out how you can incorporate a setting spray into your makeup routine–

    Application Tips and Tricks to Incorporate e.l.f. Setting Spray in Your Makeup Routine—

    Here's how you can apply the e.l.f. setting spray to get a flawless result—

    Step 1- The first and foremost step is to follow a CTM routine to prep your skin before proceeding with the next steps.

    Step 2- Now, apply a primer to ensure that the makeup lasts for the longest time while filling the pores and making the skin smooth.

    Step 3- Apply your foundation in a dotted manner. Note, that in this step, you can spritz some setting spray for a smooth base and to give your foundation a natural finish.

    Step 4- Next, you can apply concealer and blend well to camouflage all your blemishes and imperfections.

    Step 5- Apply some loose powder or a compact powder to set the liquid products followed by blusher, bronzer, and highlighter.

    Step 6- In the next and final step, you need to spray some more setting spray to set the base and get the desired finish you want out of your makeup.
