Farmer Wants A Wife's Neil Seaman reveals his tragic past

Neil Seaman made an emotional confession during Monday's episode of Farmer Wants A Wife. 

Sitting down for dinner, the 43-year-old sheep farmer thought it was time for him to open up to the girls about his tragic past.

'Half a lifetime ago, I experienced something which you wouldn't wish on anyone, which has pretty much shaped the person I am today,' he confessed.

Getting teary, Neil began to speak about his relationship with his former girlfriend, Francine, describing them as being 'inseparable'.
