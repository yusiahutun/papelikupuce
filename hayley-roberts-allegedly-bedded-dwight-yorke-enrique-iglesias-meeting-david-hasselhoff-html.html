She may be dating David Hasselhoff but it seems that isn't the first celebrity that Hayley Roberts has been with.

The former Debenhams sales assistant allegedly had a fling with two other famous faces prior to her relationship with the former Baywatch hunk.

And it seems that Hayley isn't fussy about her lover's occupation.

The sales assistant and the actor: David Hasselhoff began dating Debenhams shop worker, Hayley Roberts, seven months ago

Currently content with actor and former reality TV judge, David, the 31-year-old reportedly enjoyed a fling with footballer Dwight Yorke and singer Enrique Iglesias too, the Star on Sunday claims.

The Welsh blonde apparently enjoyed a four-month fling with Dwight that ended weeks before she met the Knight Rider star.

She met the footballer in August last year at the Living Room bar in Manchester, where the ex-Trinidad and Tobago international invited Hayley over to his table.

The pair spoke with one another until 1am and then made their way back to the sportsman's £1million mansion in Alderley Edge.

Loved up: David and Hayley, pictured here arriving at The Novello Theatre in London earlier this year, started dating after they met when the actor as a BGT judge

According to a source, their first night together was 'pretty active' but their relationship wasn't based on sex.

One of Hayley's closest pals told the Star: 'He was always sending her romantic texts, telling her how much he was missing her and couldn't wait to see her again.'

The blonde made regular trips from Wales to Manchester to spend time with Dwight, having a limo pick her up from the train station.

'He'd have someone over to cook for them or they'd go out for dinner, then have sex at his place,' the friend told the paper.

Pretty active: Footballer Dwight Yorke reportedly enjoyed a fling with Hayley after meeting her at the Living Room Bar in Manchester

However things soon fizzled out between the two of them with Hayley telling her pal that their romance was 'running out of steam.'

Her fling with Hero singer Enrique apparently occurred years ago back in 2003.

It was before the pop star had begun dating Russian tennis player, Anna Kournikova, and occurred after Hayley was mesmerised by the singer's good looks.

The former shop worker walked over to him and introduced herself and a few hours later he invited her back to his hotel room.

'Mesmerised': Hayley also bedded singer Enrique Iglesias, pictured here at the Ocean Drive Magazine Eighteenth Anniversary event in March this year, back in 2003 MIAMI, FL - MARCH 09: (Photo by Gustavo Caballero/Getty Images)

'She said that he loved her body, especially her a**e' added the close friend. 'She said they spent most of the night having sex.'

Hayley started dating U.S. star, David, seven months ago after they met when he was filming Britain's Got Talent.

The actor said he would propose to his girlfriend if Wales beat France in yesterday's Rugby World Cup semi-final yesterday...seems like Hayley will be waiting a bit longer for that diamond ring.

A spokesperson was unavailable for comment.
