BiBi – Career and Professional Life

In the year 2017, She signed with ‘Feel GHood Music’ after Yoon Mi-rae discovered her self-produced songs on SoundCloud. Therefore, she made her official debut by releasing a single “Binu” on May 15, 2019.

Her other singles include ‘Step? (2019)’, ‘Restless (2020)’, ‘Kazino (2020)’, ‘Listen 035 (2020)’, ‘I’m Good at Goodbye’s (2020)’, ‘Dingo X BIBI (2020)’, and ‘Eat My Love (2021)’.

Moreover, her other mini albums are ‘The Manual For People Who Want To Love (2019)’ and ‘Life is a Bi… (2021)’. She has also released ‘Fly with Me (2018)’, ‘The Thief Who Stole Your Heart (2018)’, and ‘Han River (2018)’.

She appeared as a contestant in the SBS show ‘The Fan’, where she finished in second place. Also, in 2021, she appeared in the ‘TVING’ show ‘Girls High Mystery Class’ as a Cast member.

Moreover, she won an award for the ‘Brand Customer Loyalty Award’ for the category ‘R&B Soul Artist’. For the same award, she was nominated for the category ‘Hot Icon Award’.

In the year 2021, she was nominated for the ‘Korean Hip-hop Awards’ for the categories ‘New Artist of the Year’, and ‘R&B Track of the Year’ for the recipient (Kazino). And, she was also nominated for ‘Collaboration of the Year’ for the recipient (Automatic Remix).
