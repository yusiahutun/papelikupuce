Cleophas Malala Discloses His New Luxurious Mansion Cost Him K Sh 200m: &quot;Nataka Kujenga Nyumba Zingi

  • Cleophas Malala revealed that his massive new house has cost him abnout KSh 200m but he is still planning to build more mansions
  • The former senator revealed that he has stayed with his wife for 19 years; he is not planning to marry a second wife

Former Kakamega senator Clephas Malala has revealed the total amount of money he has so far spent on his new humongous rural home in Makunga.

Malala's luxurious home with gold design

Malala wowed Kenyans on social media after giving them a glimpse of the Makunga home which he fit with state-of-the-art furniture, giving it a luxurious ambience.

In the photos, one could easily mistake Malala's home for a five-star hotel as the seats, chandeliers, design, and decor exude the lavishness that can only be found in some world-class hotels.

The living room for instance is the ultimate definition of luxury as the gold-themed seats, chandeliers and gypsum ceiling give the house an aura of grandeur.

Malala spents nearly KSh 200m

In an interview on Milele FM, the politician disclosed that his family moved into the mansion before its completion.

According to the father of four, the two-storey building has 12 bedrooms in total.

Malala divulged that he has spent close to KSh 200m on the mansion and he is still planning to build more mansions of the same state.

"Hiyo itakuwa rural home. Uko ndio tutakaa tukienda nyumbani sasa nni kutafuta hela ya kujenga nyumba zingine kama hizo... Sasa za retire, munataka niishi wapi."Kwa sababu sijaimaliza kwa sababu niliingia tu haraka ndio niwachie ndugu zangu zingine nafasi ya kujenga.. Sasa hivi nimetumia kama KSh 150m ikielekea KSh 200m," Malala said.

Malala planning to add more kids, not wife

Malala said he might sire more kids but he ruled out the idea of adding another wife.

"Sisi Waluhya hauwezi jua utazaa watoto wangapi. Unajenga tu alafu ukiongeza wanaingia. Nina watoto wanne peke yake lakini nimeadopt wengine watatu.Bibi ni mmoja. Unajua ukianza kukua na bibi wawili maisha yako inaanza kuenda mrama kabisa. Bibi unakua na bibi mmoja na vijana wajue kwamba nyumba inajengwa na mke mmoja, hata bibilia imeandika, kuwa na bibi mmoja tu. Na mke wangu nimekaa naye kwa ndoa kwa miaka 19 .. Nilimpata kama niko miaka 18," Malala added.
