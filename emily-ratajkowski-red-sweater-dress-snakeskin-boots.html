See Emily Ratajkowski Elevate a Red Sweater Dress with Snakeskin Boots

Emily Ratajkowski stepped out in a head-turning iteration of a classic fall styling combo.

The model was spotted on one of her signature strolls in NYC on Thursday, where she wore a perfect autumnal look courtesy of Loewe. She opted for a fiery red wool minidress, which included a plunging V-neckline, draped detailing, and flouncy long sleeves pushed up to her shoulders. The star went sans pants and added a statement pair of shimmering knee-high python boots.

The My Body author accessorized minimally with dainty stud earrings, thin gold bangles, black sunglasses, and a small black shoulder bag, and she also carried a white shoulder bag. She wore her dark-brown hair straight with a middle part, and topped off her natural makeup look with a glossy nude lip.

MEGA//Getty Images

Earlier this month, the High Low host wore another sultry spin on a cozy sweater dress. Her brown strapless midi dress featured a large bow at the top in a ribbed texture, and the skintight silhouette ended in a flowy skirt. She paired the midi with caramel-colored suede knee-high boots with stiletto heels by MVP Wardrobe, and her accessories included a black leather crossbody bag, gold hoop earrings, and black oval sunglasses by Miu Miu.

When EmRata isn't showing her enviable personal style through looks that are equally cozy and sexy, the star often steps out in a selection of perfect fall coats. Last Monday, she took the oversized trend to new heights while pairing a black double-breasted coat that was several sizes too big with loose navy trousers. She completed the casual outfit with a white tee, black sneakers, black cat-eye sunglasses, and gold hoops.
