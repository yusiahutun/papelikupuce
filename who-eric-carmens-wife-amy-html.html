How many wives did Eric Carmen have?

Throughout his lifetime, the musician was married three separate times.

Eric first tied the knot with Marcy Hill on October 31, 1978, however their marriage was short lived ending only a year later.

In 1993, he then went on to marry Susan Brown and the pair had two children - Clay and Kathryn - before splitting in 2009.

Amy and Eric walked down the aisle in 2016 and stayed together until the songwriter passed away at the age of 74 in 2024.

The couple did not have any children together over the course of their marriage.
