Don Jazzy Elated after His Crush Rihanna Welcomes Baby Boy, Fans Console Him: &quot;Don't Cry&quot;

  • Don Jazzy top Nigerian music mogul has finally reacted to the news of music star Rihanna welcoming her first child
  • News broke out on May 19, 2022, that the Barbadian singer had finally welcomed a baby boy with her boyfriend, A$AP Rocky
  • Don Jazzy, who for the longest time has been crushing on Rihanna, took to his official social media page to congratulate her

News that international singer Rihanna has finally given birth had fans on social media anticipating the reaction of music mogul, Don Jazzy.

Jazzy crushed on Rihanna

The Mavin boss has been known to have a huge crush on Rihanna and even the news of her pregnancy drew an interesting reaction from him.

However, on May 19, 2022, news made the rounds on social media that the Fenty star had finally put to bed and she and her boyfriend, A$AP Rocky, welcomed a baby boy.
