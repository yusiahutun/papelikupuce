The Best Gifts Under $100 For Mother's Day 2023

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

As Mother’s Day approaches, I can’t help but sigh as the numbers in my bank account get smaller and smaller. Let’s be real: Gift-giving can be flippin’ expensive—especially when you love to be generous and give everyone and their mother (get it?) something over the holidays. For those of us who just can’t afford to spend hundreds, there are myriad gifts under $100 that will more than do the trick.

Whether you’re a self-identified talented gift-giver or not, there are so many Mother’s Day gift ideas that are no-brainers. I know it’s hard not to feel pressure to get your mom the gift she deserves, but knowing that it’s not about how much you spend, but more so about what you get, can help ease the tension. After all, mom would probably prefer that you save your money rather than spend it all on her. Plus, you can find a surprising amount of good-quality gifts, whether they’re food boxes, skincare sets or coffee accessories, for under $100.
