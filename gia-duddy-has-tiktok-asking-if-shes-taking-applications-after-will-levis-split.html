Gia Duddy has TikTok asking if she's 'taking applications' after Will Levis 'split'

As sparks appear to be flying between one American football star and a famous singer in 2023, another couple linked to the sport looks to have called it quits. Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift had the whole world talking when Taylor showed up at his game in September. However, there may also be heartache on the field as Will Levis and his girlfriend, Gia Duddy, have reportedly split.

Will Levis, 24, is an NFL star who plays as a quarterback for the Tennessee Titans. He and his former girlfriend, TikTok influencer Gia Duddy, were together for three years before going their separate ways. Since news of their split, TikTok fans have been asking if Gia is “taking applications,” and NFL supporters are “rooting” for Will.

Will Levis and Gia Duddy’s ‘break-up’

On September 19, 2023, Pardon My Take shared an image on Twitter which showed Will Levis in his football kit with the words: “Single,” and “Titans rookie QB Will Levis is officially single, ending three year relationship with college girlfriend.”

He was previously in a relationship with TikTok star Gia Duddy. Following the Pardon My Take post, neither Will nor Gia have spoken publicly about a break-up.

After joining the Tennessee Titans in 2023, Gia reportedly had plans to follow Will to Nashville, reports Daily Mail.

They still follow each other

Despite split rumors circulating, Gia and Will are still following one another on Instagram.

With 154k followers, Will can be found at @will_levis. Gia can be found at @giaduddy with 204k.

Gia also has over 516k TikTok followers and over 15.7 million likes.

The influencer went viral during the 2023 NFL Draft and, speaking on Barstool Sports, Will joked that he’d “need a cut of this,” after Gia’s social media following “doubled” after the Draft.

Will’s ex is ask if she’s ‘taking applications’

After the couple’s reported break-up, many of their followers have been taking to the comments sections of their social media posts.

One person said that Will was “heartbroken,” while another said: “He’s single folks watch out haha.”

More wrote comments of support: “Grow closer to God in the season of singleness brother.”

Gia also had people commenting on her social media posts. One TikTok user asked: “You taking applications?” and another said: “So gorgeous. You won’t be single long, that’s for sure.
