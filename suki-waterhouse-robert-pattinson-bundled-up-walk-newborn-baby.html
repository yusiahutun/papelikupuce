Suki Waterhouse and Robert Pattinson Are All Bundled Up on a Walk With Their Newborn

So far, Suki Waterhouse and Robert Pattinson’s lives as new parents involves a lot of afternoon strolls. The stars were spotted out and about with their newborn yet again on Saturday afternoon, where they looked especially bundled up on what must have been a chilly one in Los Angeles.

For their walk, Waterhouse sported a baggy pair of black slacks and a long knit cardigan over a button-up. For extra warmth, the “Faded” singer layered another small zip-up jacket on top, and she wrapped a polka-dot-printed scarf around her head. To round out the look, she donned a pair of tiny round sunglasses and black sneakers.

BACKGRID

As for the singer’s Batman-starring beau, he looked quite cozy as well in a puffy army-green parka jacket. The Twilight alum kept the outerwear zipped up, and he pulled the hood over his head, so it sat on top of a black baseball cap. Despite being all swathed up top, Pattinson wore a pair of black cargo shorts down low, letting his legs remain bare. He rounded out the fit with bright blue and orange Nike sneakers.

The two were seen walking beside a woman who pushed the stroller holding their newborn. This sighting occurred only days after Waterhouse confirmed the sex of their baby onstage at Coachella. “Some pretty big events have been going down,” she told the audience. “I love amazing ladies, and I’ve been very lucky to have my own little amazing lady and meet the love of my life.”

A little over a week ago, the Seance actor confirmed her daughter’s arrival in an Instagram post, in which she wrote, “welcome to the world angel ❤️.” In the Polaroid photo she posted, Waterhouse was seen holding the infant in her arms. This post came only days after she and Pattinson were seen taking their new arrival for a walk in L.A., which pretty much confirmed to the world they had welcomed the little one.
