Who is Sarah Snook?

Sarah Snook is an Australian actress that has gained popularity in the entertainment industry because of her remarkable talent and beauty. She was born on December 1, 1987, in Adelaide, Australia. This means that she will be turning 35 years old as of 2022.

Sarah grew up in the suburbs of Eden Hills with her two older sisters. The actress' father was a swimming pool salesman, while her mother was an aged care provider. Sarah did not grow up with both of her parents as they divorced when she was young.

The multitalented actress went to school at St. John’s Grammar School in Belair. She was active in drama even when she was young, and she won a drama scholarship to Scotch College in Torrens Park.

She later attended the National Institute of Dramatic Arts, graduating with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in acting from the institution in 2008.

While at the National Institute of Dramatic Art, Sarah participated in theatrical productions of Gallipoli and Macbeth. She got her first television performance in an episode of All Saints in 2009. After that, she took other acting roles in movies and television shows, gradually elevating her career.

The talented actress has won numerous accolades from institutions recognising her impressive performance. She has won AACTA Award for the Best Actress for her role in Predestination. She is currently working on various projects, and the most recent is Daina Reids’ horror/thriller Run Rabbit Run.

So, who is Sarah Snook's husband? The actress married the love of her life, Dave Lawson. According to her interview with Vogue Australia, they married during the pandemic period.

At the beginning of the pandemic last year, I got locked down in Melbourne with one of my best mates, and we fell in love.
