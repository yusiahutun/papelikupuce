Orlando Bloom, 46, reveals 'sometimes' Katy Perry romance is 'really, really, really challenging'

Orlando Bloom bared his sculpted physique as he stripped down to his underwear for a tantalizing new magazine shoot.

Covering the latest issue of Flaunt, the 46-year-old dreamboat hinted at the rough spots in his romance with his fiancée Katy Perry.

'We’re in two very different pools,' he said. 'Her pool is not a pool that I necessarily understand, and I think my pool is not a pool that she necessarily understands.'

Katy is a white-hot music superstar, earning a reported $25 million salary from American Idol while juggling a top-flight residency in Las Vegas, while Orlando is now on the Amazon Prime show Carnival Row opposite model Cara Delevingne.

'Sometimes things are really, really, really, challenging. I won’t lie. We definitely battle with our emotions and creativity,' he confessed.

While grappling with the ups and downs of fame, he also tried to prevent himself from either succumbing to jealousy or incurring it in others.

One anchor for Orlando has been Buddhism, which he converted to despite being confirmed as an Anglican by the Archbishop Of Canterbury.

Buddhism 'kind of landed for me more than being confirmed in Christianity,' he said, noting: 'I respect all religions - and you can’t knock Jesus - but ultimately, I think that the responsibility of my life lies within me, and I’m not going to look outside of my life for somebody to say: "Yeah, you’re saved."'
