In Photos: Chemutai Goin, Mark Masai Dazzle at Stephen Letoos Public Wedding

  • Stephen Letoo's wedding was attended by his celebrity friends, who took to social media to share the photos
  • Chemutai Goin, Mark Masai and Anita Sonia are among the celebrities who showed up for his big day
  • The journalist tied the knot with a gorgeous lass, Irene Renoi Letoo, in a white wedding ceremony that has gone viral online

Citizen TV's Stephen Letoo has tied the knot with his love Irene Renoi Letoo.

Who attended Letoo's wedding?

Letoo has been sharing news about his public wedding online. He sent out invitations to various celebrities.

The journalist's friends showed up at his wedding, amongst them former Citizen TV's Chemutai Goin, Mark Masai, and Citizen TV's Swaleh Mdoe and Anita Sonia, to name a few.

How Letoo's white wedding went down

A video of Letoo tying the knot with his wife, Irene Renoi, also surfaced. His wife dazzled in a white gown.

The two exchanged their nuptials, declaring their undying love for each other. Irene, his wife, said:

"Chairman, today I take you as my husband till death do us apart and I love you so much."

Letoo also showered his wife with love. He said:

"I give you this ring as a sign of my love from today till forever and ever."
