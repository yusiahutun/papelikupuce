Comedian Josh Thomas makes a VERY X-rated sex confession

Josh Thomas made a very X-rated sex confession during an appearance on podcast I've Got News For You on Monday.

The Australian comedian, 35, who came out as gay in his early 20s, told host Andrew Bucklow that in his teen years he once experimented with a watermelon.

'I had sex with a lot of girls, and I liked it – like, it was fun to do that when I was a teenager. But also when I was 14, I tried to f**k a watermelon,' he revealed.
