10 Fun or Useful Things to Do on Your Android Phone When You Are Bored

Your smartphone is a virtual world in itself, and there is so much you can explore. If you find yourself bored, there are many things you can do with your Android phone to convert your boredom into fun and productive time.

Depending on your mood, you can indulge in an activity like playing games, modifying your phone, learning something new, or simply performing maintenance tasks. With the versatility of Android, the sky is the limit. So, let's look at the top ten activities you can do on your Android phone when you are bored.

1. Customize Your Android Phone

Close

As Android is an open-source operating system, it allows app developers to create advanced customization apps. To give your phone a visually appealing look, you can customize your home screen, lock screen, icons, transitions, settings, wallpaper, and much more.

Third-party apps allow you to take this customization to the next level. You can change your device launcher and can also change the notification style and notification bar. Moreover, there is also an option to smartly arrange your apps into folders to give them a clean look.

Android OS also lets you add focus modes to your device to create an environment suitable for home, work, or sleep. It customizes your phone according to your schedule and location. Here are some amazing apps that let you customize your Android phone like a pro.

2. Explore and Have Fun With Google Assistant

Close

Google Assistant is a powerful voice assistant that you can use to create reminders, control your phone to open various apps, or customize your device. If you have smart devices in your home, you can control them using the assistant. It also lets you play games and hear stories to kill boredom. You can even ask it about the new and upcoming movies in the cinemas nearby.

Have fun and ask random questions that you have in mind, and the assistant will provide you with the results. You can also practice a new language using Google Assistant. Here is a list of ways you can use Google Assistant to make your life easier.

3. Perform Essential Phone Maintenance Tasks

Close

Many of us have shifted our workspaces to our smartphones. When you are busy, you are focused on a functional task to get your work done. However, there are some leftovers after each task that you don't need anymore.

You can use your spare time by performing phone maintenance tasks. For example, you can clean up your SMS and WhatsApp inboxes to stay more productive. Usually, the messages keep piling up, and you are too busy to filter and clear the unnecessary ones.

You can also clear unnecessary system files to make the device more efficient. Here are some great apps for cleaning your Android phone.

4. Automate Your Frequent Tasks

Close

To make your life easier, you should automate the tasks you perform every day, or those things you don't want to have to remember. For example, if you are using a cellular data network, you can stop checking the data usage every day by simply applying a data usage limit. In this way, your phone automatically turns off the cellular data when that limit is reached.

The Do Not Disturb mode on your phone will snooze the unnecessary notification while you are working or sleeping. And if you are tired of using biometric or pin authentication every time to unlock your phone, just enable Smart Lock and add a trusted place where you don't need authentication.

Alternatively, you can use these automation apps to perform frequent tasks in the background.

5. Download and Play a New Game

In recent years, Android gaming has improved a lot. It is now one of the major players in the gaming industry, where hundreds of games are released on the Play Store every week. Games are a fun way to kill time, and with multiplayer games, you can interact with other users from around the world and team up with them for exciting gameplay.

The achievement and level-based games keep you involved for hours. You can also play strategy and brain development games to enhance your thinking skills. Here are some of the prettiest games available for smartphones that are worth checking out.

6. Explore New Interesting Apps on Google Play

The Google Play Store has more than three million apps spread across 40 categories, with more being added every day. It's always worth checking in to find the gems among the latest releases. The Play Store also contains lists based on top-rated free apps, top-rated paid apps, and top trending apps in your location so that you can find the best ones easily.

7. Read About Your Favorite Topics

Close

Reading improves your knowledge and helps you stay focused. In your free time, you can use your Android device to read interesting stories and informational articles. Just open the Google app, and it will show you a list of stories based on your interests and location.

You can also use other apps such as Google News to explore various topics of your choice. There are some great third-party apps available too on Android that you can use for reading, like Medium, Quora, and Wattpad.

8. Learn a New Skill or Language

Close

The best way to utilize your free time is to use it for self-development. You can learn a new skill using your Android phone that will help you in your life.

As the modern workspace is skill-oriented, skill-based knowledge will empower you to overcome great hurdles. You can use YouTube to learn a skill, and there are third-party apps such as Udemy and Coursera that offer courses from top experts.

You can also learn a new language through courses from these apps, or you can download dedicated language-learning apps like Memrise and Duolingo.

9. Check Out Your Old Emails, Social Media Posts, and Photos

Close

When you are busy, you ignore most of your emails that are not related to work. Your free time is ideal for catching up on the rest. Who knows, you might find something useful. Besides, it will also help you clean your email inbox.

You can also check out your old photos and social media posts to revive your memories. You might connect with your old friends by sharing those memories with them. It is also a good opportunity to review and delete any embarrassing social media posts of the past.

10. Explore the World With Google Earth and Street View

Close

Through your Android phone, you can explore any city or area in the world. Google Earth and Google Street View are among the most popular Google inventions and are used by millions of people globally. Google Earth lets you view any location through a satellite image view or a 3D map view. It will also give you a description of the location.

With Street View, you can see a 360 view of any street in any city in the world. If you are planning to go on a trip, use Street View to explore the place first.

Kill Time and Enjoy These Activities

Your Android device has huge potential. By exploring it to its full extent, you can become more productive and efficient. All of these activities have some benefits for you, so rather than wasting your time in boredom, try these activities to get the best out of your free time.
