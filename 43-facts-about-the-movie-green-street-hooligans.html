Green Street Hooligans is a cult classic movie that delves into the gritty world of football hooliganism in London. As Matt becomes a member of the Green Street Elite, one of the most notorious hooligan firms in the city, he discovers a brotherhood like no other but also the consequences that come with it. FAQs

Q: Is Green Street Hooligans based on a true story?

A: While the movie draws inspiration from real-life events and the football hooligan subculture in England, it is not a direct adaptation of a specific true story.

Q: Who are the main characters in Green Street Hooligans?

A: The movie features Charlie Hunnam as Matt Buckner, Elijah Wood as Pete Dunham, and Claire Forlani as Shannon Dunham.

Q: Why is the movie titled “Green Street Hooligans”?

A: The title refers to Green Street, a real-life street in East London notorious for its association with football hooliganism.

A: The movie is rated R for strong brutal violence, language, and some drug use.
