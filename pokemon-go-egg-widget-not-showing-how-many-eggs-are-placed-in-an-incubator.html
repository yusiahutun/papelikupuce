Pokmon GO Egg Widget Not Showing How Many Eggs are Placed in an Incubator

As modern times are taking over everything, software, and games comply with everything that should be a standard. Now, when a widget doesn’t work at this time, we all know that something isn’t right.

Many of us are using the iOS widget for Pokémon GO, which shows how many Eggs we have now placed in Incubators. It is a tool that makes our gameplay easier and more fun. We do not have to open the app a million times to check the progress with Eggs, how many are on the way to hatch, and whatnot.

However, when a widget doesn’t work, and it is all blank, we have to do that in order to see what is happening.

Several players, who are iOS users, have said that their Egg widget is totally blank, i.e. showing a black screen (or image). All of them tried everything. From removing the widget to force-stopping the app and even reinstalling Pokémon GO, nothing has helped so far.

Currently, there are no words from Niantic on this problem, but we do hope that everything will be solved as soon as possible.
