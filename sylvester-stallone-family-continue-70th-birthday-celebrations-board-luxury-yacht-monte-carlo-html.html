Sylvester Stallone and family continue his 70th birthday on luxury yacht in Monte Carlo

It's hard to believe the Rocky legend turned 70 on Wednesday.

But Sylvester Stallone is making sure he really enjoys his milestone birthday as he continues his celebration with his family in the South of France.

On Saturday, the Hollywood star, his glamorous wife Jennifer Flavin, 47, and their three beautiful daughters were spotted boarding a mega-yacht in Monte Carlo.

Still the champ: Sylvester Stallone is enjoying a luxury European vacation to celebrate turning 70 last Wednesday and on Saturday the party continued on board a luxury yacht in Monte Carlo

The actor, looking decades younger than his actual age, showed off his barrel chest and muscled arms in a blue short-sleeved polo shirt and blue Nike shorts plus white sneakers as he carried his bag onto the yacht.
